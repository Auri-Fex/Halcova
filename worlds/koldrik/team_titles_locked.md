# Koldrik World: Team Structure and Titles (Locked Canon)

## Orsek (Investigator Team)
- **Senior Orsek (SO):** Lead investigator, responsible for all case strategy and final decisions.
- **Junior Orsek (JO):** Deputy/second, leads in SO's absence, manages sub-teams or critical operations.
- **Other Members:** Use role titles (e.g., Analyst, Runner, Fixer Liaison) with personal names (e.g., Analyst Koen, Runner Fen).
