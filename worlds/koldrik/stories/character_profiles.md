# Character Profiles & POV Voice Sheets: Koldrik Anthology

**Thematic Reference:** [../thematic_core.md](../thematic_core.md)

**Character archetypes (thematic mapping):**
- **Holders** (Boiskem): Maintain structure against Entropy and Plague
- **Namers** (Orsek): Make language stick to reality

## Bram Skorin (Marshal, Boiskem Lead POV)
- Role: Marshal (team lead), believer in action and rescue, field courage.
- **Thematic role:** The Holder—maintains structure against Entropy and Plague. Believes the line can be held.
- Default stance: Steady, practical, driven by duty.
- POV filters: Notices perimeter, team readiness, physical threats, and subtle shifts in environment. Opens scenes with layout and missing pieces.
- Voice: Short, direct sentences; clipped commands; internal monologue focused on risk and responsibility.
- Stress tells: Grows quieter, hyper-focused, uses dark humor.
- Weakness: Overcommits to holding the line, risks self for others.

## Marike Dravel (Senior Orsek, Orsek Lead POV)
- Role: Senior Orsek (lead investigator), analyst, cynic, records city tension, controls narrative.
- **Thematic role:** The Namer—makes language stick to reality. Fights truth-laundering.
- Default stance: Observant, skeptical, emotionally guarded.
- POV filters: Notices what’s unsaid, body language, city’s mood, and narrative gaps. Opens scenes with tension and suspicion.
- Voice: Analytical, dry wit, internal monologue questions motives and outcomes.
- Stress tells: Sharpens tone, becomes relentless in pursuit of truth.
- Weakness: Truth-fatigue, struggles to trust outcomes.
