# Racial Hierarchy in the Oracle Age

**Foundation**: European racial supremacy justified by oracle technology

**Key Claim**: "Oracle predictions prove racial hierarchy is natural"

**Reality**: Oracle reflects existing racism; perpetuates it through surveillance

---

## The Hierarchy (1870s Greymouth)

### Top: European (British/Anglo-Saxon)

**Position**: Dominant race; system designed for them

**% Population**: ~60% of Greymouth

**Oracle Narrative**:
- "Low crime probability proves moral superiority"
- "Success in oracle industry proves intellectual capacity"
- "We invented oracle technology" (FALSE - stolen from indigenous)

**Actual Advantages**:
- Bureau under-polices European neighborhoods
- Oracle Houses hire Europeans preferentially
- Education access (Technical Sybari)
- Legal system favors Europeans

---

### Second Tier: Other Europeans (Irish, German, Italian, Eastern European)

**Position**: Discriminated against but assimilable

**% Population**: ~15%

**Prejudice**:
- Irish: "Drunken, violent, criminal"
- Italian: "Mafia, organized crime"
- Eastern European: "Backwards, superstitious"

**Oracle Justification**:
- Higher crime probability (Bureau surveils these neighborhoods more)
- "Cultural deficiencies" blamed for oracle predictions

**Path to Acceptance**:
- Assimilation (speak Common, adopt Anglo-Saxon norms)
- Second generation "becomes white" (if successful)
- Marry into Anglo-Saxon families

---

### Third Tier: East Asian (Chinese, Japanese)

**Position**: "Perpetual foreigners"

**% Population**: ~5-7% (survivors of genocide)

- "Savage, primitive, violent"

- **They invented oracle technology** (truth erased)

---

### Outside Hierarchy: Black/African

**Position**: Small population but heavily discriminated

**% Population**: <2% (recent immigration, escaped slaves from Americas)

**Prejudice**:
- All stereotypes from slavery/colonialism

## Indigenous Peoples - Special Case

**They created oracle technology** (Kael'nir, Tav'mora, Shen'kali traditions)

**Europeans stole it** (1520s-1650s, torture and genocide)

**Now Europeans claim**:
- "We invented it"
- "Indigenous peoples too primitive to have developed this"
- "Oracle proves they're inferior"

**Using indigenous knowledge to oppress indigenous descendants**

---

### Bureau Targeting

**Indigenous Neighborhoods** (Tidemark, parts of Factory District):
- 80% crime probability (Bureau standard)
- Daily patrols (10+ agents constant presence)
- Precrime arrests common (hundreds annually)
- Curfews (10 PM, violators arrested)

**Indigenous Individuals**:
- Registered (like Sarah Blackwater, 15,000 in Greymouth)
- Weekly check-ins required
- Travel restrictions
- Employment limits (can't work government jobs)

---

### Cultural Criminalization

**Traditional Practices = Crimes**:
- Speaking Kael'nir/Tav'mora/Shen'kali in public: Arrest
- Practicing traditional oracle rituals: Arrest + execution
- Teaching children indigenous language: Arrest + children removed
- Gathering in groups >5: Suspected conspiracy, arrest

**Result**: Indigenous culture underground or dying

## Immigrant Communities

### Chinese (Largest Non-European Group)

**Why They Came**: Railroad work, mining, labor

**Where They Live**: Chinatown (part of Tidemark, separate enclave)

**Bureau Treatment**:
- High surveillance (opium den raids)
- "Tong" suspected (organized crime)
- Routine harassment

**Community Response**:
- Mutual aid societies (tongs, benevolent associations)
- Separate economy (Chinese-owned businesses)

**Resistance**: Economic success despite discrimination; maintain cultural identity
