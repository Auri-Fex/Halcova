# Alchemy: The Arcane-Punk Energy System

**Core Concept**: Alchemy is the energy/fuel/power source that runs oracle devices and industrial machinery in the 1860s-1880s. It's alien, dangerous, and extracted from the same sources as prophecy crystals—but serves a completely different function.

**Key Distinction**: 
- **Oracles** = prophecy/temporal perception (spiritual/informational)
- **Alchemy** = power/energy/fuel (physical/industrial)
- Both use crystals, but in fundamentally different ways

---

## What Is Alchemy?

**Alchemy is the industrial harnessing of resonance energy from specially prepared substances to create power.**

### The Two Forms

**1. Natural Alchemy (Indigenous)**
- Extract resonance from living/organic materials (plants, fungi, minerals)
- Sustainable, renewable, low-output
- Used for: heating, lighting, small-scale power
- Methods: fermentation, distillation, careful extraction
- **Examples**: Luminescent fungi cultures, crystal-charged water, resonance oils

**2. Industrial Alchemy (European)**
- Extract resonance from crystals through destructive processes
- High-output, non-renewable, toxic byproducts
- Used for: steam engines, oracle device power, factory machinery
- Methods: burning, crushing, acid dissolution of crystals
- **Examples**: Crystal slag fuel, resonance steam, alchemical batteries

---

## How Alchemy Works

### The Resonance Energy Principle

**All matter contains latent resonance energy** (like potential energy in physics)

**Crystals contain concentrated resonance** (formed over millennia)

**Alchemical processes release this energy** for industrial use:

**Indigenous Method**:
- Gentle extraction (fermentation, distillation, ritual preparation)
- Preserves crystal structure; can be recharged/renewed
- Low energy output but sustainable
- **Analogy**: Like tapping a maple tree (renewable harvest)

**European Method**:
- Violent extraction (burning, crushing, acid bath)
- Destroys crystal structure; single-use fuel
- High energy output but depleting
- **Analogy**: Like clear-cutting a forest (destructive harvest)

**Applications**:

## The Alchemical Industry

### Crystal Refineries

**Process**:
1. **Mining**: Extract raw crystals from Windbreak Range
2. **Sorting**: Oracle-grade crystals (prophecy) vs. fuel-grade crystals (alchemy)
3. **Refinement**: 
   - Oracle crystals: cut, polish, calibrate (preserve structure)
   - Fuel crystals: crush, dissolve in acid, distill resonance oil (destroy structure)
4. **Byproducts**: Crystal slag (burned for fuel), resonance ash (toxic waste), acidic runoff

**The problem**: 
- Same mines supply both oracle industry AND alchemical industry
- As crystals deplete, competition increases
- By 1870s, most crystals go to alchemy (higher profit; industrial demand)
- Oracle industry suffering (crystal quality declining)

---

### Alchemical Products (1870s Market)

**Premium**:
- **Resonance Oil** (refined; clean-burning; expensive): £5/vial
  - Used by: Oracle Houses, wealthy homes, precision machinery
  - Glows pale blue; almost no smoke; slight humming sound when burning

**Standard**:
- **Crystal Spirits** (distilled; industrial-grade): £1/vial
  - Used by: Factories, steam engines, middle-class lighting
  - Glows amber; produces fumes; smells metallic

**Cheap**:
- **Slag Fuel** (crushed crystal waste): 5 shillings/pound
  - Used by: Poor heating, low-grade engines, desperation
  - Barely glows; thick black smoke; toxic

**Illegal**:
- **Wild Extract** (indigenous sustainable alchemy): Priceless/banned
  - Made from: Living fungi, charged water, ritual preparation
  - Renewable but low-output; Bureau criminalizes possession

## Indigenous vs. European Alchemy

### Indigenous Sustainable Alchemy

**Philosophy**: "Take only what renews; give thanks for what you take"

**Methods**:
- **Fungal cultivation**: Grow luminescent mushrooms (natural resonance)
- **Crystal-charged water**: Place crystals in spring water (absorbs resonance; renewable)
- **Ritual distillation**: Ferment sacred plants with crystal presence (low-yield but renewable)

**Output**: Low but sustainable
- Enough for: lighting, small heating, ritual purposes
- Not enough for: industrial machinery, mass production

**The irony**: Indigenous alchemy could power sustainable society; Europeans dismissed it as "primitive"

---

### European Industrial Alchemy

**Philosophy**: "Extract maximum value; efficiency über alles"

**Methods**:
- **Acid dissolution**: Dissolve crystals in sulfuric acid (releases all resonance at once)
- **High-temperature burning**: Burn crystal slag at extreme heat (100% extraction)
- **Mechanical crushing**: Pulverize crystals to expose more surface area (faster extraction)

**Output**: High but destructive
- Enough for: Steam engines, factories, oracle power, urban lighting
- Cost: Crystals destroyed, sacred sites depleted, toxic waste everywhere

**The cycle**:
1. Mine crystals (destroy sacred sites)
2. Burn crystals for power (toxic pollution)
3. Use power to mine more crystals (perpetual extraction)
4. Deplete sources (system collapsing by 1870s)

## Alchemy & Oracle Technology (How They Connect)

### The Critical Difference

**Oracles use crystals FOR resonance** (detect temporal patterns)
**Alchemy uses crystals AS fuel** (burn resonance for energy)

### Both Need Same Resource (The Conflict)

**The competition**:
- Limited crystal supply (mines depleting)
- Oracle industry wants: pristine crystals (prophecy quality)
- Alchemical industry wants: bulk crystals (burn for power)
- By 1870s: Alchemy winning (more profitable)

**Result**: Oracle quality declining
- Fewer premium crystals available
- Oracle Houses forced to use lower-grade materials
- Accuracy dropping year by year (40% → 35% → 30% by 1880s)
- Indigenous peoples warning: "You're burning your future to power your present"

### Why Europeans Don't Care

**Short-term thinking**:
- Alchemy = immediate profit
- Oracle quality = long-term problem
- "We'll find more crystals" (but there aren't more)

**Class division**:
- Rich can still afford premium oracle devices (use their crystals)
- Poor suffer declining accuracy (use cheap crystals)
- "Market will solve it" (it won't)
