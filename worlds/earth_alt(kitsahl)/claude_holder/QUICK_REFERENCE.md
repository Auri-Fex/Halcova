## CURRENCY (Quick Conversion)

**1 Sovereign** = 20 Shillings = 240 Pence ≈ £1
**1 Oracle Credit** = 5 Sovereigns

**What Things Cost**:
- Loaf of bread: 3-6 pence
- Week's rent (Tier 5): 2-4 shillings
- Oracle consultation: 50-200 Sovereigns
- Factory wage: 8-12 shillings/week
