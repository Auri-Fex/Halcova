# KNALVOSSAK — Primary City Arena

## Why It Exists

Knalvossak sits on the **last reliable hardground lip** where the state can meter flow before Jitknal turns navigation into probability.

**Trade Name:** Knalvoss
**Street Name:** Voss
**Suffix:** -ak (chartered commission seat)

---

## City Anatomy (Three-Layer Model)

### Layer 1: LOCK LADDER
The physical infrastructure that controls water flow.

**Components:**
- **Gates:** Primary flow control
- **Teeth:** Engagement mechanisms
- **Braces:** Structural reinforcement
- **Spillways:** Overflow management
- **Governors:** Pressure regulation

### Layer 2: REGISTRY SPINE
The bureaucratic infrastructure adjacent to physical chokepoints.

**Components:**
- **Commission offices:** Schedule and closure authority
- **Seal benches:** Validity and admissibility decisions
- **Assay stations:** Grade verification

### Layer 3: TOW/MARKET BELT
The economic ecosystem wrapped around throughput.

**Components:**
- **Labor markets:** Tow crews, maintenance, handlers
- **Repair yards:** Hull work, device service
- **Food and lodging:** Supporting the transient population
- **Street economy:** Everything that serves people waiting

---

## The Power Triangle

### CANAL COMMISSION
**Authority:** Schedules, closures, gate priority, emergency protocol
**Power:** They decide when things move
**Weapon:** Closure orders (WC/QC/SC/EC)

### SEAL HOUSES
**Authority:** Identity, validity, cargo truth, admissibility
**Power:** They decide what counts as real
**Weapon:** Admissibility reviews and holds

### ORDER COMPANIES
**Authority:** Contract coercion; seizure and escort as paid services
**Power:** They make enforcement happen
**Weapon:** Detain receipts and evidence collection

## Daily Rhythms
