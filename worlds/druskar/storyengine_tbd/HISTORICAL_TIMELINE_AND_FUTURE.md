## DEEP HISTORY (Sketched)

### Pre-Canal Era
- Salkorin settlement patterns follow water
- Overland routes exist but unreliable (cold shelf conditions)
- Early chronomass discovery and wild harvesting
- Oddswork emerges as folk practice

### Canal Construction Era
- First engineered waterways connect natural water bodies
- Lock technology develops
- Puljitos emerges as canal-state concept
- Knalvossak founded at hardground lip

### Standardization Era
- Chronomass farming systematized (Kronvar Belt)
- Seal House system established
- Commission bureaucracy formalized
- Order Companies emerge as enforcement contractors
