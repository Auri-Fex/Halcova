## Questions to Answer Before Writing

### Political Questions
1. What specific event disrupts the status quo?
2. Which faction benefits from the disruption?
3. What evidence exists of the disruption's cause?
4. Who has motive to cause/prevent resolution?

### Character Questions
1. Who witnesses the inciting incident?
2. What do they have to lose?
3. What skills make them capable of acting?
4. What relationships complicate their choices?

### System Questions
1. Which magic system is most relevant to Book 1?
2. What forensic evidence drives investigation?
3. What bureaucratic obstacle creates urgency?
4. What chokepoint is contested?

### Tone Questions
1. Is Book 1 primarily investigation, action, or political?
2. What's the emotional arc of Book 1?
3. What's the darkest moment?
4. What qualifies as victory?
