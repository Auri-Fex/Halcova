# Major Locations Gazetteer

## 1) SELMARA

**sel-mara** — "Saltwind City"

### Classification

Major coastal port; Standard Enclave hub; treaty-port noir city

### Geography

* Coastal location in Selina (Saltwind Littoral)
* Deep harbor with industrial docks
* Terraced hillside districts
* Warehouse labyrinth near waterfront

### Economy

* Major trade hub for Charged Pale imports
* Refinery district (cracking stillworks on riverboats)
* Telegraph nexus with multiple repeater stations
* Seal Syndic headquarters district

### Key Districts

**The Standard Enclave** (Central Treaty Zone):
* Hardware-enforced boundaries
* Weapons checked at gates
* Insurance-backed security
* Diplomatic quarter

**Foundry Row**:
* Industrial kilns and forges
* Varnish accumulation hazard
* Dense worker housing
* High creature outbreak risk

**The Tangles**:
* Warehouse district
* Smuggling corridors
* Seal counterfeit markets
* Syndic patrol routes

**Crown Square**:
* Wealthy residential
* Seal Syndic offices
* Neutral Pier chambers
* Telegraph exchanges

**Admiralty Heights**:
* Harbor oversight offices
* Naval inspection facilities
* Signal Pale concentration
* Latchhand training sites

**Tidemark**:
* Dock worker slums
* Bilge-level housing
* Varnish Sheet infestations
* Desperate labor markets

### Story Hooks

* Counterfeit Charged Pale investigation
* Seal Syndic corruption case
* Warehouse district outbreak
* Dockworker uprising
* Telegraph intelligence theft

## 2) KHARRAS

**khar-ras** — "Pit Reach"

### Classification

Major terrace metropolis; extraction hub; vertical city

### Geography

* Terraced crater city in Kharveth (Pale Pit Country)
* Vertical districts descending into pit
* Pale seam access at multiple depths
* Claim Ring territories mark extraction zones

### Economy

* Pale Medium extraction (primary)
* Pressure apparatus manufacturing
* Claim defense and security
* Vertical transport infrastructure

### Key Districts

**Upper Terraces**:
* Broker headquarters
* Claim registry offices
* Wealthy extraction families
* Spine Broker branch sites

**Mid Rings**:
* Worker housing
* Equipment markets
* Maintenance shops
* Pressure vessel foundries

**Deep Pits**:
* Active extraction sites
* Hazardous seep zones
* Armed claim guards
* Contamination hotspots

**The Sealed Terrace** (Off-Limits):
* 15-year-old outbreak zone
* Pale-Gilled infestation
* 200+ deaths
* Rumors of survivors
* Entry forbidden

### Story Hooks

* Claim jumping violence
* Pressure wake accident
* Sealed Terrace expedition
* Pale-Gilled nest purge
* Extraction sabotage

## Quick Location Selection Guide

### For Port Noir Stories

**Primary**: Selmara (major city, all institutions present)

**Secondary**: Selveth (smaller scale, regional focus)

### For Frontier Violence Stories

**Primary**: Kharras (terrace wars, claim jumping)

**Secondary**: Vethgoro (deeper, more desperate)

**Tertiary**: Shetmar (desert boom/bust)

### For Corridor Crisis Stories

**Primary**: Tulwarda (major lock, high stakes)

**Secondary**: Nartul (regional lock, local focus)

### For Spine Broker Stories

**Primary**: Girveth (established monopoly)

**Secondary**: Shetmar (temporary sites, instability)

### For Horror Outbreak Stories

**Any location works**, but:
* **Urban**: Selmara warehouse district
* **Vertical**: Kharras Sealed Terrace
* **Corridor**: Tulwarda spillways
* **Frontier**: Vethgoro deep pits
