# Capital Cities

> **Quick Summary**: The five regional capitals range from Kalen-Sheloth's 900,000 (largest, wealthiest, political center) to Konor's ruined camps of 30,000 desperate survivors.

---

## Kalen-Sheloth (Tavanor)

**Population**: 900,000 (largest on continent)
**Ruler**: Shalen-Mor (High Archon)

**Districts**:
- Palace Quarter (elite, 50,000)
- Academic District (university, libraries, 100,000)
- Commercial Center (markets, banking, 150,000)
- Industrial Zone (factories, refineries, 200,000)
- Slums (tenements, disease, 400,000)

**Features**:
- Sky Beacon (navigation landmark)
- Council Hall (nine barons meet)
- Arcane Academy (1,000 students)
- Rail hub
- Undercity (ruins beneath)

**Economy**: Government, academia, trade, banking
**Dangers**: Political assassinations, Breakers bombings, class riots, Inquisitor presence

---

## Vorn-Merath (Sulenor, on Eshtor border)

**Population**: 400,000
**Ruler**: Koresh-Val

**Zones**:
- Fortress-Palace (Koresh-Val's stronghold, 10,000)
- Refinery Complex (40% of continental shyak, 50,000 workers)
- Forge-City (weapons manufacturing, 100,000)
- Slave Markets (largest on continent, 20,000)
- Barracks (5,000 soldiers)
- Worker Tenements (220,000)

**Features**: Toxic air, brutal conditions, invasion staging ground
**Economy**: Extraction, refining, weapons, slavery
**Dangers**: Worker revolts (crushed), air poisoning, Extraction Wars frontline

---

## Thren-Kalgash (Vashor)

**Population**: 50,000
**Ruler**: Threnok-Gash

**Structure**: Carved into glacier, mostly underground

**Quarters**:
- Ice Fortress (Threnok-Gash, 5,000)
- Mercenary Bazaar (hiring hall, 10,000)
- Ice-Creature Pens (volatile, 5,000)
- Underground City (carved ice, 30,000)

**Economy**: Mercenary contracting, ice-creature extraction, trade
**Dangers**: Extreme cold, paranoid purges (weekly executions), creature escapes

---

## Dresh-Kalmor (Eshtor)

**Population**: 200,000
**Ruler**: Dreshak-Mor

**Layout**: Built on stilts over swamps, connected by canals

**Districts**:
- Baron's Platform (center, 10,000)
- Smuggling Docks (black market, 50,000)
- Vice District (brothels, drugs, gambling, 40,000)
- Worker Stilt-Houses (100,000)

**Economy**: Swamp-creature extraction, smuggling, vice, corruption
**Dangers**: Disease (malaria-equivalent 40% infection), crime universal, no law

---

## Meshira-Tal's Ruins (Konor)

**Population**: 30,000 (down from 400,000)
**Ruler**: Meshira-Tal (desperate)

**Status**: 90% abandoned ruins, refugee camps in rubble

**Zones**:
- Former Palace (partially standing, Meshira-Tal, 1,000)
- Refugee Camps (tents and hovels, 29,000)
- Salvage Areas (scavenging, dangerous)
- Dead (bodies everywhere)

**Economy**: NONE (imports everything, can't pay)
**Dangers**: Entropy storms (daily), starvation (200+ deaths/week), cannibalism (confirmed), violence (constant)
