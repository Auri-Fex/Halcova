# Major Historical Events

> **Quick Summary**: Key moments that shaped Norbela's history, from ancient collapses to the Second Cascade to current crises.

---

## Ancient Era (Pre-1500)

**The Lost Continents** (Unknown date):
- Seventh and eighth continents consumed by dead zones?
- Complete population extinction
- Evidence: Ancient texts, ruins
- Debate ongoing

**The First Kingdoms** (~1000):
- Regional powers emerge
- Baron system precursors
- Cultural foundations

---

## Discovery Era (1500-1600)

**Discovery of Shyak** (~1515):
- Concentrated magical oils found
- Changes everything
- Miracle to industry

**Treaty of the Coasts** (1562):
- First international agreement
- Divides creature territories
- Sparks wars

---

## Industrial Era (1600-1872)

**The First Crash** (1700-1750):
- Resource collapse
- Residue storms
- Economic depression
- Forced adaptation

**Treaty of Crystal Caverns** (1762):
- Nine powers agree to quotas
- Conservation (theoretical)
- Baron council framework

**The Second Cascade** (1872):
- 500,000 km² instant dead zone
- 100,000+ killed
- Government relocated
- World changed forever

---

## Current Era (1872-Present)

**Konor Collapse** (1850-present):
- 60% dead zones
- Population 750K → 200K
- Ongoing catastrophe

**Northern Vanishings** (~1895):
- 10,000 people disappeared
- Five settlements empty
- Nethvor suspected
- Investigation ongoing

**Gen-Sorath Siege** (Current):
- Inquisition besieging city
- 80,000 starving
- 1 month in
- Telumesh Archive threatened

**Extraction Wars** (1885-present):
- Koresh-Val invading Eshtor
- Velasha-Nen defending
- Civilian massacres
- Ongoing conflict

**Meshira-Tal's Ultimatum** (Current):
- Searching for superweapon
- Demands aid or threatens use
- Countdown: Months
- Council refuses
