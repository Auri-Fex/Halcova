# Device Catalog

> **Quick Summary**: Comprehensive catalog of arcane devices by category—from household lamps to industrial machinery to experimental weapons—with costs, fuel consumption, and failure rates.

---

## Lighting Devices

### Arcane Lamps (Standard)
**Function**: Produces steady light  
**Cost**: 50-200 GS  
**Fuel**: 0.1 liters/hour  
**Reliability**: 95%  
**Access**: All classes

### Arcane Streetlights
**Function**: Public illumination  
**Cost**: 500 GS (municipal)  
**Fuel**: 0.5 liters/hour  
**Reliability**: 90%  
**Access**: Cities only

### Portable Lanterns
**Function**: Handheld light source  
**Cost**: 20-50 GS  
**Fuel**: Fauxil acceptable  
**Reliability**: 70% (fauxil), 90% (shyak)  
**Access**: All classes

---

## Communication

### Message Stones (Paired Crystals)
**Function**: Voice communication, ~100km range  
**Cost**: 500-2,000 GS per pair  
**Fuel**: 1-2 liters/hour  
**Reliability**: 85%  
**Access**: Upper/middle castes

**Limitations**:
- Must be paired (factory process)
- Signal blocked by entropy zones
- Conversations not private (can be intercepted)

### Scrying Mirrors
**Function**: Visual communication, ~50km range  
**Cost**: 10,000+ GS  
**Fuel**: 3-5 liters/hour  
**Reliability**: 70% (requires skilled operator)  
**Access**: Barons, military only

**Limitations**:
- Image fuzzy
- Requires line-of-sight (metaphysically)
- Very expensive to maintain

### Arcane Telegraph Terminals
**Function**: Text transmission via network  
**Cost**: Municipal infrastructure  
**Fuel**: 0.5 liters per message  
**Reliability**: 80% (network dependent)  
**Access**: Public (monitored)

---

## Transportation

### Arcane Carriages
**Function**: Self-propelled vehicles  
**Speed**: 30-50 km/h  
**Range**: 200km per tank (20 liters)  
**Cost**: 5,000-20,000 GS  
**Reliability**: 75%  
**Access**: Upper castes

**Maintenance**: 500-1,000 GS/year

### Arcane Rail Cars
**Function**: Train transport  
**Speed**: 80-100 km/h  
**Fuel**: 100-500 liters per journey  
**Ticket**: 10-50 GS (class-based)  
**Reliability**: 70% (delays common)  
**Access**: All classes (segregated)

### Skyships (Experimental)
**Function**: Aerial vessels  
**Speed**: 40-60 km/h  
**Range**: 500km per tank  
**Cost**: 2,000,000 GS (prototype)  
**Reliability**: **50% crash rate**  
**Access**: Military, desperate only

## Weaponry

### Arcane Firearms
**Type**: Musket-equivalent  
**Range**: 50-100 meters  
**Rate**: 1 shot per 10-15 seconds  
**Cost**: 200-500 GS  
**Ammo**: 1 GS per shot  
**Reliability**: 90%  
**Access**: Military, mercenaries, elites

**Effect**: Instant death on torso hit; cauterized wounds

### Arcane Cannons
**Type**: Artillery  
**Range**: 500-1,000 meters  
**Payload**: Explosive shyak or entropy bombs  
**Cost**: 50,000-200,000 GS  
**Fuel**: 50-100 liters per shot  
**Reliability**: 85%  
**Access**: Military only

**Effect**: 10-50 meter kill radius

### Arcane Blades
**Type**: Enhanced melee weapons  
**Enhancement**: Superior cutting, cauterizes  
**Cost**: 500-5,000 GS (quality-based)  
**Fuel**: Infused during forging (permanent)  
**Reliability**: 98%  
**Access**: Officers, nobles, elite mercenaries

### Entropy Grenades (BANNED)
**Type**: Handheld entropy weapons  
**Range**: 20m kill radius, 50m mutation radius  
**Cost**: 5,000-10,000 GS (black market)  
**Effect**: Horrific mutations, slow agonizing death  
**Reliability**: 95% (too reliable)  
**Access**: Black market only

## Medical

### Arcane Surgery Kits
**Function**: Seal wounds, mend bones, remove tumors  
**Cost**: 5,000-20,000 GS  
**Fuel**: 1-10 liters per procedure  
**Success Rate**: 80% (skilled), 40% (unskilled)  
**Access**: Keshiel-Circle healers, elite physicians

### Regeneration Tanks
**Function**: Regrow limbs, organs  
**Cost**: 100,000+ GS  
**Fuel**: 50-200 liters per treatment  
**Success Rate**: 60%  
**Access**: Barons only

**Failures**: Limbs grow wrong, painful, useless

### Life Extension Apparatus
**Function**: Slow aging via shyak infusions  
**Cost**: 50,000 GS (device) + 5,000-10,000 GS/year (fuel)  
**Effect**: +20-40 years if maintained  
**Side Effects**: Translucent skin, visible organs, pain  
**Access**: Ultra-wealthy only

## Industrial

### Arcane Forges
**Function**: Smelt metal at extreme heat  
**Fuel**: 100-500 liters/day  
**Cost**: 50,000-200,000 GS  
**Reliability**: 80%  
**Access**: Industrial operations

### Arcane Looms
**Function**: Weave fabric, 10x productivity  
**Fuel**: 20-50 liters/day  
**Cost**: 10,000-50,000 GS  
**Reliability**: 85%  
**Access**: Textile factories

### Arcane Pumps
**Function**: Drain mines, irrigate, supply water  
**Fuel**: 10-100 liters/day (size-dependent)  
**Cost**: 5,000-50,000 GS  
**Reliability**: 75%  
**Access**: Industry, municipal

### Arcane Automata (Experimental)
**Function**: Self-operating machines  
**Tasks**: Lift, carry, dig, simple assembly  
**Cost**: 50,000+ GS  
**Reliability**: **60% (40% go berserk)**  
**Access**: Telvor-Shin's labs only

## Household (Upper Caste)

### Climate Control Systems
**Function**: Heat or cool rooms  
**Cost**: 10,000-50,000 GS  
**Fuel**: 5-20 liters/day (cooling 10x heating)  
**Reliability**: 85%  
**Access**: Elite only

### Arcane Lifts (Elevators)
**Function**: Vertical transport in buildings  
**Speed**: 1-2 meters/second  
**Cost**: 50,000-100,000 GS per installation  
**Fuel**: 0.5 liters per trip  
**Reliability**: 90%  
**Access**: Tallest buildings only

### Self-Cleaning Surfaces
**Function**: Shyak-infused materials repel dirt  
**Cost**: 2x normal material cost  
**Fuel**: Infused (slowly depletes)  
**Reliability**: 95%  
**Access**: Wealthy homes

## Security and Surveillance

### Arcane Eyes (Surveillance Spheres)
**Function**: Record/transmit visual data  
**Range**: 100 meters  
**Cost**: 1,000+ GS each  
**Fuel**: 0.5 liters/hour  
**Reliability**: 80%  
**Access**: Government, barons, wealthy

**Easily destroyed**: Thieves know to look for them

### Intrusion Wards
**Function**: Magical alarms, detect unauthorized entry  
**Coverage**: Building or room  
**Cost**: 5,000-50,000 GS  
**Fuel**: 1 liter/day  
**Reliability**: 85%  
**Access**: Wealthy districts

**Bypassed**: Nethvor-Shadows has countermeasures

### Shyak Detectors
**Function**: Sense magical oil, find smugglers  
**Range**: 50 meters  
**Cost**: 500-2,000 GS  
**Reliability**: 70% (many false positives)  
**Access**: Authorities, customs

---

## Forbidden Devices (Black Market)

### Slave Control Collars
**Function**: Shock/choke disobedient slaves  
**Cost**: 500-1,000 GS  
**Fuel**: Slagcells (rechargeable)  
**Reliability**: 90%  
**Status**: Illegal but common

### Counterfeit Shyak Synthesizers
**Function**: Dilute/corrupt oil, sell as pure  
**Cost**: 10,000-50,000 GS  
**Profit**: 100x markup  
**Reliability**: 95% (too good at crime)  
**Status**: Highly illegal

### Soul Transfer Apparatus
**Function**: Move consciousness between bodies  
**Cost**: 500,000+ GS  
**Success Rate**: **1%**  
**Failures**: Screaming flesh-piles  
**Status**: Death penalty for possession

### Necromantic Animator
**Function**: Animate corpses  
**Cost**: 50,000-100,000 GS  
**Product**: Zombies (stupid, decay), specters (insane)  
**Reliability**: 70% (animation), 0% (control)  
**Status**: War crime

## Experimental/Prototype

### Entropy Reversal Device
**Function**: Theoretically reverse dead zones  
**Cost**: Billions (never successfully built)  
**Status**: Theory only  
**Risk**: 80% chance causes worse cascade

### Shyak Synthesis Machine
**Function**: Create artificial magical oil  
**Cost**: 10 million GS spent (Telvor-Shin)  
**Success Rate**: **0%**  
**Status**: Impossible (magic inherent to life)

### Corruption Chamber
**Function**: Deliberately expose to entropy, create super-soldiers  
**Cost**: 100,000+ GS  
**Success Rate**: 20% (80% die screaming)  
**Result**: Berserkers (strong, insane, short-lived)  
**Status**: War crime
