# Vice and Addiction

> **Quick Summary**: 15-20% of population is substance-dependent, with drugs, alcohol, gambling, and sex work providing escape from brutal reality—or paths to worse exploitation.

---

## Alcohol

**Upper Castes**:
- Fine wines, arcane spirits
- Recreational use
- Rarely addiction (can afford treatment)

**Middle Castes**:
- Beer, ale
- Social drinking
- Alcoholism emerging (stress)

**Lower Castes**:
- Moonshine, rot-gut
- Epidemic alcoholism
- Only escape from misery

---

## Drugs

### Shyak Derivatives

**Pure Shyak** (upper castes):
- Injected/inhaled
- Extremely addictive
- Hallucinogenic, euphoric
- Enhances magic
- Causes magical dependency + physical addiction

**Shirok** (low-grade):
- Workers use for stamina
- Jitters, insomnia, heart damage
- Common in factories

**Shivesh** (corrupted):
- Outcasts use
- Causes mutations, madness, entropy corruption
- Fatal long-term

### Herbal Drugs

- Painkillers (opium-equivalent)
- Stimulants
- Hallucinogens
- Legal but regulated

### Addiction Crisis

**Statistics**: 15-20% dependent

**Consequences**:
- Theft, violence, prostitution
- Overdoses daily (lower castes)
- Children born addicted
- No treatment for poor

**Control**: Nethvor-Shadows + corrupt officials; immense profits

---

## Gambling

**Upper Castes**: High-stakes (fortunes won/lost)  
**Middle Castes**: Tavern games (dice, cards)  
**Lower Castes**: Street gambling (predatory, debt traps)

**Pipeline**: Gambling debts → indenture → slavery

---

## Sex Work

### Legal Status

- Regulated in cities
- Taxed
- Guild-controlled (exploitative)

### Tiers

**Upper-Tier**: Courtesans for elite; well-paid; some autonomy; still exploited

**Mid-Tier**: Brothels for middle/lower; guild-controlled; workers indentured

**Low-Tier**: Street work; unregulated; dangerous; survival prostitution

**Dark Reality**: Child exploitation widespread, unpunished among elites
