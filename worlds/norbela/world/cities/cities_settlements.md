# Cities & Settlements of Norbela

Genre: Dark Fantasy / Arcane Punk / Hard R
Scope: Major cities, regional hubs, frontier towns, and abandoned sites across five regions.
Use: Locations with governance, economy, hazards, travel, and ready-to-run hooks.

---

## Continental Overview
- Regions: Tavanor (academia/seat of power), Sulenor (volcanic extraction), Eshtor (forests), Vashor (frozen north), Konor (dying desert)
- Travel Baselines: Foot: 20–30 km/day; Horse/Carriage: 80–100 km/day; Arcane Carriage: ~60 km/h; Rail: ~100 km/h; Skyship: ~80 km/h (direct)
- Class Access: Upper castes enjoy safe transit; lower castes risk bandits, guards, and device failures.
- Hazards: Entropy dead zones (40% of continent), banditry, inquisitorial checkpoints, industrial accidents, weather extremes.

## Tavanor

### Kalen-Sheloth (Capital) — Pop. ~900,000
- Government: Shyakorin Council seat; High Archon’s palace; ministries by caste.
- Economy: Finance, governance, high magitech manufacture; elite services; propaganda.
- Districts: White Spires (government); Brass Quarter (magitech workshops); Low Steps (slums); Red Courts (vice); Archivists’ Row.
- Landmarks: Council Rotunda; Nine-Baron Plinth; Blue Sun Observatory; Shyak Exchange.
- Hazards: Assassinations, protests, refinery-linked bombings, rolling blackouts in poor districts.
- Hooks: Protect a delegate ahead of the succession vote; track Nethvor agents in the Brass Quarter; evacuate Low Steps during a pipeline rupture.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A sprawling, tiered arcane punk capital city. At the highest point, grand government towers and elite mansions (White Spires, Blue Sun Observatory) overlook the city. Below, concentric rings of magitech workshops (Brass Quarter), archives, and bustling markets surround the Council Rotunda and Nine-Baron Plinth. Further down, vice districts (Red Courts), open-air markets, and dense slums (Low Steps) descend toward pipeline yards and polluted river docks. Rail lines and skyship towers connect districts. Pollution, magical glow, and class barriers are visible. Moody lighting, blue and brass tones, birds-eye view, highly detailed.

### Kel-Tavanor (Arcane Grid City) — Pop. ~220,000
- Role: Power-plant metropolis feeding Kalen-Sheloth; site of past meltdown (dead zone NE).
- Economy: Power generation, conductor-crystal fabrication, battery reconditioning.
- Governance: Grid Council (arcanists + ministers); corrupt energy monopolies.
- Hazards: Reactor surges, crystal conduit fires, scavengers breaching exclusion zones.
- Hooks: Stabilize a failing reactor; expose an energy rationing scam; recover bodies from the exclusion zone for grieving families.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
An industrial arcane city built around massive power plants and conductor-crystal towers. The city core is dominated by reactors and arcanist guild halls, surrounded by worker housing and battery markets. The northeast is scarred by a dead zone from a past meltdown. Rail lines, power conduits, and exclusion fences are prominent. Smog, arcane glow, and security checkpoints define the landscape. Birds-eye view, moody, blue-white energy highlights.

### Tava-Shoren (Scholastic City) — Pop. ~120,000
- Role: Collegium-town for engineers, historians, and arcanists.
- Economy: Education, publishing, instrument-making, consulting.
- Culture: Debate duels; restricted stacks; student guild politics.
- Hooks: Debate turns deadly with entropy blades; steal a banned text before Inquisitors arrive; quell a student strike that Nethvor funds.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A collegiate city with a central campus of arcane towers, libraries, and debate halls. Student housing, artisan shops, and publishing houses form the middle ring. The outskirts feature guild lodges and protest camps. Statues, banners, and magical wards are visible. Lively, intellectual, but tense atmosphere. Birds-eye view, detailed, with magical and academic motifs.

## Sulenor

### Vorn-Merath (Refinery Metropolis) — Pop. ~400,000
- Role: Heart of shyak refining; permanent smog; endless shifts.
- Economy: Refining, component casting, heavy transport.
- Hazards: Explosions, vat deaths, gang wars over fuel lines.
- Hooks: Whistleblower needs escort with proof of slave vat deaths; sabotage ring escalates beyond intent; negotiate a ceasefire between rival refinery crews.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A dense, smog-choked industrial city. The core is dominated by refinery towers, magitech guilds, and the Baron’s Keep. Surrounding rings of worker housing, market squares, and smog theaters. The outer city is lined with slums, waste pools, and pipeline yards. Rail depot and collapsed refinery ruins are visible. Gritty, polluted, with arcane energy and firelight. Birds-eye view, dark and industrial.

### Korin-Delash (Smelter City) — Pop. ~180,000
- Role: Ore-to-part pipeline for magitech frames and weapons.
- Governance: Smelter Syndic oligarchy (answer to Koresh-Val).
- Hazards: Slag landslides, furnace collapses, corrupt inspectors.
- Hooks: Stop a furnace collapse mid-shift; uncover counterfeit alloy in arcane rifles; free children trapped in a slag tunnel.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A city built around massive smelters and ore-processing plants. The core features furnace towers and oligarch mansions. Mid-rings have worker barracks, markets, and guild halls. The outskirts are marked by slag heaps, collapsed tunnels, and shantytowns. Rail lines and ore carts crisscross the city. Harsh lighting, metallic tones, and visible pollution. Birds-eye view, industrial and hazardous.

### Sulen-Telk (Mining Town) — Pop. ~35,000
- Role: Deep-shaft extraction near volcanic vents; temp labor camps.
- Hazards: Gas bursts, tunnel monsters, foreman brutality.
- Hooks: Rescue a trapped crew; expose shyak dilution; decide whether to arm miners for a strike.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A mining town at the edge of volcanic vents. Central shaft towers, labor camps, and foreman offices dominate the core. Surrounding are rows of worker tents, supply depots, and makeshift markets. The outskirts are scarred by gas vents, collapsed tunnels, and monster-haunted ruins. Dim, smoky, with red-orange arcane glow. Birds-eye view, rugged and tense.

## Eshtor

### Velash-Korin (Conservation Stronghold) — Pop. ~150,000
- Role: Administrative seat for protected forests; Vornalen-Keepers influence.
- Economy: Sustainable harvests, herbal medicine, arcane inks.
- Hazards: Border raids by extractionists; informants; eco-sabotage.
- Hooks: Broker ceasefire with Koresh raiders; relocate endangered creatures through a war zone; root out a Nethvor cell stoking both sides.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A city built in and around protected ancient forests. The core features the Keeper’s Hall, council ring, and warded groves. Mid-rings have herbalist markets, healer enclaves, and artisan homes. The outskirts blend into logging camps, border watchtowers, and eco-sabotage sites. Magical wards, ancient stones, and lush greenery contrast with industrial encroachment. Birds-eye view, green and gold tones, mystical atmosphere.

### Shor-Emeth (Frontier Logging) — Pop. ~28,000
- Role: Contested town at the edge of protected tracts.
- Hazards: Ambushes, device snares, corrupt magistrates.
- Hooks: Defuse a show trial for “eco-terrorism”; dismantle deadly arcane traps; protect a harvest that won’t trigger a cascade.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A frontier town at the edge of contested forests. Central logging yards, magistrate’s hall, and device shops form the core. Outlying camps, barricades, and eco-traps ring the town. The border is marked by stumps, regrowth, and magical wards. Tense, rugged, with visible conflict between nature and industry. Birds-eye view, earthy and arcane details.

### Eshto-Vel (Forest Sanctuary) — Pop. ~9,000
- Role: Hidden enclave of healers and Keepers; warded groves.
- Hazards: Inquisitorial purges, shyak sickness among refugees.
- Hooks: Smuggle medical supplies past checkpoints; evacuate the enclave before a siege; debate saving a criminal who can cure shyak poisoning.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A hidden enclave deep in warded groves. Central healing halls, shrines, and Keeper dwellings are surrounded by magical barriers and refugee camps. The outskirts blend into dense, enchanted forest. Inquisitorial patrols and sick refugees are visible at the edges. Peaceful but tense, with glowing wards and natural beauty. Birds-eye view, soft green and blue light.

## Vashor

### Vash-Keloth (Ice Port) — Pop. ~70,000
- Role: Northern trade, whaling, salvage from frozen ruins.
- Hazards: Icequakes, raiders, entropy-flare auroras.
- Hooks: Hunt missing ice caravans; negotiate with salvage gangs; seal a rift over the bay.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A northern port city on frozen shores. Central trade halls, whaling docks, and salvage yards cluster near the icy bay. Outlying districts include worker barracks, market squares, and arcane signal towers. The outskirts are marked by ice fields, entropy rifts, and aurora-lit ruins. Cold, stark, with magical blue-white glow. Birds-eye view, wintry and industrial.

### The Five Silent Towns (Abandoned)
- Mer-Vash, Teth-Moren, Shy-Kaleth, Vor-Shalin, Kelor-Vash — all went dark over two months.
- Status: Empty homes, warm meals left uneaten; ritual signs in basements; thrall patrols at night.
- Hooks: Retrieve family registers for survivors; escort Moreth-Kiren’s evidence south; plant beacons to track ritual energy and survive the hunt.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
Five abandoned towns, each with empty homes, unfinished meals, and ritual markings in basements. Streets are patrolled by thralls at night. The outskirts are overgrown or frozen, with signs of sudden flight. Eerie, haunted, with magical residue and entropy scars. Birds-eye view, desolate and ominous.

### Frostline Stations (Waystations) — Pop. 200–800 each
- Role: Caravan posts spaced 50–80 km apart.
- Hazards: Whiteouts; corrupt guards; shyak heater failures.
- Hooks: Repair heaters during a blizzard; arbitrate a murder among trapped travelers; fend off a thrall pack until dawn.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
Small, isolated waystations spaced along frozen caravan routes. Central heater towers, bunkhouses, and supply sheds are surrounded by snowdrifts and arcane signal posts. The outskirts are marked by whiteouts, broken sleds, and lurking threats. Cold, lonely, with magical blue glow. Birds-eye view, minimalist and stark.

## Konor

### Meshir-Atal (Desert Capital) — Pop. ~110,000 (and falling)
- Role: Seat of Meshira-Tal; ultimatum city.
- Economy: Aid depots, rationing, relic salvage from buried cities.
- Hazards: Sand-corrupted shyak; famine riots; sabotage.
- Hooks: Decide who receives final water shipments; intercept an entropy bomb convoy; evacuate a hospital before generators fail.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A desert city built around a central palace, aid depots, and water cisterns. Mid-rings feature markets, ration lines, and salvage yards. The outskirts are famine slums, sand-worn ruins, and caravan camps. Entropy bomb vaults and collapsed aqueducts are visible. Harsh, sun-bleached, with magical shimmer and dust. Birds-eye view, golden and blue tones.

### Konor-Veld (Caravan Hub) — Pop. ~60,000
- Role: Trade nexus between oases; mercenary hiring hall.
- Hazards: Sandstorms, slave-catchers, poisoned wells.
- Hooks: Guard a multi-faction caravan; expose well-poisoners; free press-ganged refugees.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
A bustling caravan hub at the crossroads of desert oases. Central trade halls, mercenary hiring yards, and market squares dominate the core. Outlying districts include tent camps, stables, and water wells. The outskirts are marked by sandstorms, slave pens, and poisoned wells. Lively but desperate, with magical energy and dust. Birds-eye view, warm and chaotic.

### Dune Holdfasts (Clans) — Pop. 300–1,200 each
- Role: Semi-nomadic settlements around ancient cisterns.
- Hazards: Cistern collapses, sandwraiths, Council tax raids.
- Hooks: Stabilize a cracked cistern; mediate clan blood feud; decide whether to reveal a hidden oasis.

#### Text-to-Image Prompt
Semi-nomadic clan settlements clustered around ancient cisterns. Central tents, water storage, and communal fires are surrounded by sand dunes, makeshift defenses, and hidden oases. The outskirts are threatened by sandwraiths and Council patrols. Rugged, sun-baked, with magical blue-green highlights. Birds-eye view, intimate and survival-focused.
