## üéØ The 12 Macro Categories

Your wiki will be organized into these top-level categories:

1. **PLANETARY** - Physical world, celestial mechanics, climate
2. **LORE** - History, mythology, major events, timeline
3. **SOCIETY** - Culture, daily life, customs, social structures
4. **POLITICS** - Government, factions, conflicts, power
5. **GEOGRAPHY** - Continents, cities, regions, natural features
6. **MAGIC** - Creatures, finyak, shyak, magical systems
7. **TECHNOLOGY** - Devices, engineering, infrastructure
8. **ECONOMICS** - Trade, currency, resources, industries
9. **RELIGION** - Faiths, beliefs, spiritual practices
10. **CHARACTERS** - Major figures, biographies, relationships
11. **LANGUAGE** - Conlang, naming conventions, linguistics
12. **REFERENCE** - Quick guides, indexes, tools

Each category will have a landing page and 6-12 wiki pages.

## ‚úÖ Quality Standards

### Every Wiki Page Must Have:

**Frontmatter** (YAML metadata):
```yaml
---
title: "Page Title"
macro_tags: [PRIMARY, SECONDARY]
region_tags: [Regions]
era_tags: [Time periods]
status: Complete | In Progress | Stub
word_count: Approximate count
last_updated: YYYY-MM-DD
related_pages: [Links]
source_files: [Original files]
---
```

**Content Structure**:
- Quick summary (1 sentence)
- Table of contents
- Overview (2-3 paragraphs)
- Main sections (3-6)
- "In the World" section
- "Story Integration" section
- "Related Topics" section
- References and metadata

**Quality Metrics**:
- No contradictions
- Proper conlang usage
- Appropriate tone
- 1500-5000 words (major) or 500-1500 (minor)
- 3-5 plot hooks minimum
