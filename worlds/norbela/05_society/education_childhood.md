# Education and Childhood

> **Quick Summary**: Education and childhood experiences vary from privileged tutoring to no childhood at all, with caste determining everything from birth.

---

## Education by Caste

### Upper Castes

**Private tutors** from birth  
**Elite academies** (Telumesh, Baron's Court Schools)  
**Curriculum**: History, politics, economics, arcane theory, warfare, diplomacy, arts  
**Outcomes**: Leadership positions guaranteed

### Middle Castes

**Guild apprenticeships** (ages 10-18)  
**Basic academics** + trade skills  
**Curriculum**: Specialized by guild, rigorous  
**Outcomes**: Professional positions if successful; Korbal if fail

### Lower Castes

**Public schools** (ages 6-10, if accessible)  
**Basic literacy** + arithmetic  
**Curriculum**: Minimal, obedience focus  
**Outcomes**: Slightly better jobs than illiterate; still trapped

### Indentured/Slaves/Outcasts

**No formal education**  
**Job skills** (if owner trains)  
**Literacy rare**, seen as unnecessary/dangerous

---

## Childhood by Caste

### Upper Castes

**Birth**: Celebrated; heirs are political assets  
**Childhood**: Sheltered, educated, no manual labor  
**Coming of Age**: 16; formal ceremonies; positions in family  
**Expectations**: Marry well, consolidate power

### Middle Castes

**Birth**: Celebrated if affordable  
**Childhood**: Basic education, learn trade at 8-10  
**Coming of Age**: 14; apprenticeship or military  
**Expectations**: Master trade, marry within class

### Lower Castes

**Birth**: Often unwanted; burden  
**Childhood**: Work begins at 6, frequent hunger  
**Coming of Age**: 10-12; childhood ends  
**Expectations**: Survive, contribute

### Indentured/Slaves

**Birth**: Property  
**Childhood**: Labor from age 4-5  
**Separated**: From mother at 5-10  
**Expectations**: Obey, work, die quietly
