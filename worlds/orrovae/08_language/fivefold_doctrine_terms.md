## OVERVIEW

The Fivefold Doctrine is the dominant theological framework governing Säide-host relationships in Orrovae. To write authentic dialogue, characters must use the correct terminology—both mainstream Orthodox terms and Finno-Ugric variants used by Unseeded communities.

This document provides the language characters would actually use when discussing symbiote relationships, religious concepts, and the Maiming Wars.

## DOCTRINAL PHRASES BY CHARACTER TYPE

### Orthodox Believers (The Unmaimed, Traditionalists)

**Blessings & Greetings**:
- "Muster sei mit dir" — *Pattern be with you* (standard greeting)
- "Im Faden verbunden" — *Bound in the Thread* (formal)
- "Ganz und gesund" — *Whole and healthy* (well-wishing)

**Warnings & Curses**:
- "Sein Muster ist gebrochen" — *His Pattern is broken* (euphemism for Hollowing)
- "Fadenlos" — *Threadless* (severe insult: no lineage worth preserving)
- "Ohne Schwur" — *Without Covenant* (untrustworthy)

**Religious Expressions**:
- "Das Muster prüft" — *The Pattern tests* (explanation for hardship)
- "Der Faden erinnert sich" — *The Thread remembers* (Thread-Memory concept)
- "Die Ganzheit verlangt" — *Wholeness demands* (justification for sacrifice)

**The Unmaimed Specific**:
- "Unversehrt bleiben" — *Remain unmaimed* (their greeting)
- "Die Prüfung zeigt" — *The test reveals* (core doctrine: maiming reveals weakness)
- "Muster-stark" — *Pattern-strong* (high compliment)
- "Muster-schwach" — *Pattern-weak* (severe judgment)

### Plain Condition Adherents (Vabakoda, Unseeded Communities)

**Daily Speech**:
- "Eheys on sisältä" — *Wholeness is within* (greeting)
- "Liitto on oma" — *The Covenant is your own* (self-determination)
- "Kuvio ei ole kohtalo" — *The Pattern is not fate* (rejection of genetic destiny)

**Counter-Doctrine**:
- "Säije ei sido" — *The Thread does not bind* (rejection of lineage obligation)
- "Kumppanuus vai orjuus?" — *Partnership or slavery?* (questioning symbiosis)
- "Eheys ilman säiettä" — *Wholeness without Thread* (life is complete without Säide)

**Identification**:
- "Vapaa" — *Free* (self-description)
- "Tyhjä" — *Empty* (what Seeded call them; reclaimed)
- "Oma-eheys" — *Self-whole* (term of pride)

### Säide-Writs (Medical/Religious Professionals)

**Technical Terms**:
- "Verbundschaft-Störung" — *Partnership disorder* (symbiotic dysfunction)
- "Schwur-Bruch" — *Covenant breach* (boundary violation)
- "Muster-Erosion" — *Pattern erosion* (genetic degradation)
- "Faden-Riss" — *Thread-tear* (lineage damage)
- "Ganzheit-Verlust" — *Wholeness-loss* (Hollowing in progress)

**Procedural Language**:
- "Die Siegelung" — *The Sealing* (initial bonding ceremony)
- "Schwur-Erneurung" — *Covenant renewal* (annual ritual)
- "Muster-Prüfung" — *Pattern testing* (genetic assessment)
- "Faden-Lesung" — *Thread reading* (lineage consultation)

## COMMON DIALOGUE EXPRESSIONS

### By Social Tier

**Crownwalk (Elite)**:
- "Unser Faden reicht zurück bis..." — *Our Thread reaches back to...*
- "Ein makelloses Muster" — *A flawless Pattern*
- "Die Verbundschaft meines Hauses..." — *The Partnership of my House...*

**Midworks (Professional)**:
- "Mein Schwur ist mein Wort" — *My Covenant is my word*
- "Arbeiten wie im Muster" — *Working as in the Pattern* (doing things right)
- "Faden-treu" — *Thread-true* (reliable)

**Underdeck (Working Class)**:
- "Kein Muster, keine Sorge" — *No Pattern, no worry* (fatalistic)
- "Der Faden hält—oder nicht" — *The Thread holds—or doesn't*
- "Ganzheit ist für die da oben" — *Wholeness is for those above*

### Exclamations & Oaths

**Positive**:
- "Muster sei Dank!" — *Pattern be thanked!*
- "Beim Faden!" — *By the Thread!* (mild oath)
- "Ganzheit gefunden!" — *Wholeness found!* (relief)

**Negative**:
- "Muster-verflucht!" — *Pattern-cursed!*
- "Fadenschnitt!" — *Thread-cut!* (severe curse)
- "Zur Höhle!" — *To the Hollow!* (damn it)

**Neutral/Surprised**:
- "Was sagt der Faden?" — *What does the Thread say?*
- "Schwur und Siegel!" — *Covenant and Seal!*

## THE UNMAIMED: RITUAL LANGUAGE

### Initiation Phrases

**Arbiter (Cynewulf)**:
"Oletko valmis todistamaan Kuvion?" — *Are you ready to witness the Pattern?*

**Initiate Response**:
"Näytä minulle Totuus." — *Show me the Truth.*

**Binding Words**:
"Sinun Musterin on sinun Tuomiosi. Sinun Säijeesi on sinun Perintösi. Unversehrt bleiben."
*Your Pattern is your Judgment. Your Thread is your Heritage. Remain Unmaimed.*

### Trial Ceremony

**Before Testing**:
"Das Muster prüft alle. Die Starken bleiben. Die Schwachen fallen."
*The Pattern tests all. The strong remain. The weak fall.*

**After "Successful" Test**:
"Unversehrt. Muster-stark. Der Faden dauert fort."
*Unmaimed. Pattern-strong. The Thread continues.*

**After "Failed" Test**:
"Enthüllt. Das Muster hat gesprochen."
*Revealed. The Pattern has spoken.*

### Daily Affirmations

**Morning**:
"Heute werde ich das Muster ehren. Heute werde ich unversehrt bleiben."
*Today I will honor the Pattern. Today I will remain unmaimed.*

**Before Meals**:
"Diese Nahrung stärkt mein Muster. Diese Stärke dient dem Faden."
*This food strengthens my Pattern. This strength serves the Thread.*

**Before Sleep**:
"Mein Schwur wacht über mich. Mein Muster hält."
*My Covenant watches over me. My Pattern holds.*

## DIALOGUE SAMPLES

### Orthodox Believer
> "Das Muster hat ihm diese Prüfung gegeben, und er hat versagt. Sein Faden war schwach—das ist jetzt klar. Besser, dass wir es wissen, bevor er unsere Kinder mit seinem schwachen Blut verunreinigt."

*"The Pattern gave him this test, and he failed. His Thread was weak—that's clear now. Better we know before he pollutes our children with his weak blood."*

### Vabakoda Elder
> "Eheys on sisältä, lapsi. He sanovat, että olemme tyhjiä, mutta me olemme täynnä—täynnä itseämme, ei vierasta olentoa. Säije ei sido meitä, koska me emme tarvitse sitä."

*"Wholeness is within, child. They say we are empty, but we are full—full of ourselves, not a foreign creature. The Thread does not bind us because we do not need it."*

### Cynewulf Preaching
> "Die Schwachen schreien, dass wir grausam sind. Aber wir sind nur ehrlich. Das Muster lügt nicht. Der Faden lügt nicht. Wenn jemand zerbricht—haben wir ihn zerbrochen? Oder haben wir nur enthüllt, was immer war?"

*"The weak cry that we are cruel. But we are only honest. The Pattern does not lie. The Thread does not lie. When someone breaks—did we break them? Or did we only reveal what always was?"*

### Sigurd Internal Monologue
> "Verbundschaft-Instabilität in den unteren Schichten der Kolonie. Der Schwur hält noch, aber die Erosion ist messbar. Wenn ich das Muster stabilisieren kann, ohne dass er es merkt... aber nein. Er verdient die Wahrheit."

*"Partnership instability in the lower layers of the colony. The Covenant holds, but the erosion is measurable. If I can stabilize the Pattern without him noticing... but no. He deserves the truth."*
