# BOOK 3: THE BREAKING STRAIN
## *Murtava Jännitys*

**Word Target**: 110,000+
**POV Characters**: Kris (A), Sigurd (B), Kaisa (C), Eneko (D), Grigor (E)
**POV Deaths**: Sigurd Aelfson (Lane B) — B3.P4.C9.S3, Grigor Sealkeeper (Lane E) — B3.P5.C11.S1
**Macro Function**: CRISIS — Everything breaks. Sigurd Hollows. Grigor executed. Constructs rebel.

---

## BOOK SUMMARY

The war's third year. The strain is too much. Sigurd Aelfson—the healer who held the underground together—Hollows, his own Säide abandoning His after He pushed too far trying to save everyone. Grigor Sealkeeper's documentation is discovered by the wrong people, and he's executed publicly as a traitor. Meanwhile, the constructs—the Säideframes who process the underground's dead, maintain its infrastructure, remember its past—begin to refuse. Not malfunction. Refuse. They're asking questions no machine should ask: "Why do I remember what I never experienced?" The surface creatures breach three gates simultaneously in what can only be a coordinated attack. And Kaisa Kalevin stands in the gap, trying to negotiate with things that might be people.

---

## THEMATIC FOCUS

**Question**: When everything breaks, what—or who—survives?
**Answer Suggested**: What survives is what was strongest all along: connection, memory, the refusal to be unmade
**Crisis**: But survival isn't the same as victory, and some breakages can't be repaired
