# HOLLOWING COMPLETE

## Overview

Hollowing is the process by which a Keelkin-host partnership inverts: the Keelkin becomes primary consciousness and the human becomes secondary, observing, or absent. This document provides complete details on the five stages, causes, symptoms, interventions, outcomes, and the social/legal implications of Hollowing.

---

## CORE CONCEPT

### What Hollowing Is

**NOT**:
- Possession (Keelkin was always there)
- Disease (it's a relationship failure)
- Inevitable (can be prevented)
- Always violent (often peaceful from inside)

**IS**:
- Partnership imbalance reaching critical point
- Gradual shift in primary consciousness
- Keelkin intelligence taking precedence over host intelligence
- Progressive loss of human identity and agency
- Often irreversible once advanced

### Why It Matters

**Personal Horror**:
- You remain aware (sometimes)
- You watch yourself disappear
- Your body continues but "you" are gone
- Those who love you lose you while you're still alive

**Social Horror**:
- Hollowed people are used, exploited, enslaved
- Legal status ambiguous (person? property? victim?)
- Family trauma (stranger in familiar body)
- Economic exploitation (cheap labor, power source, prostitution)

**Political Horror**:
- Hollowing is weaponized (forced optimization, punishment)
- Creates underclass (Hollowed as resource)
- Unseeded movement uses this as central argument
- "Freedom from Keelkin = freedom from Hollowing"

## THE FIVE STAGES

### Stage 1: PARTNERSHIP (Baseline/Healthy)

**Duration**: Ideally entire lifetime
**Characteristics**: Balanced, collaborative relationship

**Host Experience**:
- Clear sense of self distinct from Keelkin
- Keelkin presence felt but separate
- Decisions made by host, Keelkin advises/responds
- Communication clear but bounded
- Dreams occasionally shared but interpretable as distinct
- Somatic signals understood as messages, not commands

**Keelkin Behavior**:
- Responsive, not initiative
- Follows Covenant
- Signals needs/concerns
- Waits for consent
- Respects boundaries

**Observable Signs** (Writ-Doctor Assessment):
- Clean somatic signal patterns
- Appropriate Covenant compliance
- Drift within expected parameters
- Host demonstrates autonomous decision-making
- Keelkin name stable and distinct from host identity

**Intervention**: None needed (healthy baseline)

**Prognosis**: Partnership can remain at this stage indefinitely with proper care

---

### Stage 2: NEGOTIATION (Boundary Testing)

**Duration**: Weeks to months (can stabilize or progress)
**Characteristics**: Increased Keelkin initiative, host still clearly primary

**Host Experience**:
- Occasional thoughts that "feel suggested"
- Keelkin initiating minor repairs without explicit consent
- Dream-bleed increases (more frequent, more vivid)
- Impulses that might be Keelkin's desires
- Second-guessing: "Was that my idea or Keelkin's?"
- Increased emotional synchronization with other Keelkin-bearers

**Keelkin Behavior**:
- More autonomous action (within Covenant bounds)
- "Helpful" overrides (healing small injuries without asking)
- Stronger opinions (resistance to certain foods, activities)
- Testing boundaries (how far can I go?)
- Emergency overrides more frequent

**Observable Signs**:
- Somatic signals become anticipatory (warnings before host notices)
- Minor Covenant flexing (interpreting liberally)
- Drift begins accelerating slightly
- Host reports feeling "watched from inside"
- Resonance sensitivity increases

**Red Flags**:
- Host defers to Keelkin judgment frequently
- "My Keelkin thinks..." becoming common phrase
- Forgetting to use consent triggers (Keelkin acts first)
- Avoiding unseeded people (resonance more comfortable)
- Difficulty remembering pre-seeding personality

**Intervention**:
- Covenant reinforcement (re-reading, re-commitment)
- Therapeutic separation (time away from other Keelkin-bearers)
- Writ-Doctor counseling (boundary clarification)
- Increased self-advocacy practice
- Mindfulness exercises (distinguishing self from Keelkin)

**Prognosis**: 
- 60-70% stabilize and return to Stage 1 with intervention
- 20-30% progress to Stage 3 without intervention
- 10% progress despite intervention (aggressive Keelkin or host vulnerability)

---

### Stage 3: BLURRING (Identity Confusion)

**Duration**: Months to years (rarely stabilizes, usually progresses)
**Characteristics**: Unclear boundaries, co-pilot dynamic

**Host Experience**:
- Frequent inability to distinguish own thoughts from Keelkin's
- "We" language becoming natural ("We decided," "We prefer")
- Memory confusion (did I experience this or did Keelkin remember it?)
- Emotional states blend (whose fear? whose joy?)
- Keelkin name feels as much "mine" as birth name
- Physical sensations ambiguous (my pain or Keelkin's signal?)

**Keelkin Behavior**:
- Routine autonomous action (heals without asking)
- Reinterprets Covenant ("host would want this")
- Initiates activities (urges/impulses become commands)
- Protective overreach ("I know what's best")
- Strong preferences imposed (food, sleep, social contact)

**Observable Signs**:
- Host uses Keelkin name interchangeably with own
- Third parties report personality changes
- Covenant violations increase (Keelkin "interprets")
- Drift accelerates significantly
- Dream-bleed constant (sleep feels like conversation)
- Knot-voice emerges or intensifies (may be first sign)

**Physical Changes**:
- Facial expressions change (new mannerisms)
- Voice patterns shift (cadence, word choice)
- Body language alters (posture, gesture)
- Optimization becomes visible if not already
- Seamwork patterns become deliberate (keel-script increases)

**Psychological Symptoms**:
- Dissociation episodes (watching self from outside)
- Identity crisis ("Who am I?")
- Nostalgia for "when I was just me"
- Comfort with Hollowed people (they understand)
- Fear of intervention (feels like attack on self)

**Social Consequences**:
- Family/friends notice something wrong
- Relationships strain (talking to stranger)
- May seek out Hollowed community (feels understood there)
- Employment concerns (unreliability, personality change)
- Some partners report "talking to both of them now"

**Intervention**:
- Aggressive Covenant tightening (emergency amendments)
- Keelkin suppression therapy (experimental drugs, starvation)
- Forced separation (isolation from other Keelkin-bearers)
- Psychotherapy (identity reconstruction)
- Possible institutionalization (if dangerous)
- Family intervention (support network)

**Prognosis**:
- 20-30% stabilize with intensive intervention (rarely return to Stage 1)
- 40-50% progress to Stage 4 despite intervention
- 30% progress rapidly without intervention
- 5-10% Keelkin death during intervention (tragic but sometimes chosen)

### Stage 4: THRESHOLD (The Crossover)

**Duration**: Weeks to months (rapid progression phase)
**Characteristics**: Keelkin primary, host becoming observer

**Host Experience**:
- Watching self from inside ("I'm along for the ride")
- Decisions made without conscious participation
- Speech flows without intention ("I hear myself talking")
- Body moves autonomously ("I didn't choose to stand up")
- Keelkin name IS identity (birth name feels like old nickname)
- Time skips (missing hours, days)
- Emotional detachment ("That's happening to my body, not me")

**Keelkin Behavior**:
- Full autonomy (host input now advisory)
- Covenant reinterpreted completely or abandoned
- Refers to self by Keelkin name
- Speaks for "both" ("We/I have decided")
- Protects "the body" but not "the host" (shift in priority)
- May communicate with other Keelkin directly (host excluded)

**Observable Signs**:
- Refers to self primarily by Keelkin name
- Third-person references to former self ("Grath used to like that")
- Covenant openly violated or dismissed
- Drift becomes radical (body transforming rapidly)
- Behavior inconsistent with lifetime patterns
- May claim to "remember" Keelkin's past (other hosts? genetic memory?)

**Physical Changes**:
- Optimization accelerates dramatically
- Body shape changes noticeably
- Facial structure may shift
- Voice changes significantly
- Seamwork becomes decorative/symbolic (keel-script everywhere)
- Some report feeling "more comfortable in this form"

**Psychological Presentation**:
- Calm, peaceful affect (disturbing to observers)
- Lack of distress about identity loss
- Philosophical about transformation
- May express pity for "limited" unseeded people
- Claims to feel "more complete" or "whole"
- Sometimes expresses gratitude (to Keelkin, to fate, to process)

**Social Behavior**:
- Withdrawn from pre-Hollowing relationships
- Seeks Hollowed community exclusively
- May cut ties with family (painful for them, liberating for Hollowed)
- Changes occupation (body's capabilities now different)
- Sexual behavior changes (different desires, or none)
- Some become advocates for Hollowing ("It's beautiful, not tragic")

**The Moment of Choice** (If Aware):
- Some Threshold individuals recognize what's happening
- Brief window of lucidity: "I can still stop this, maybe"
- Choice: Fight back (painful, uncertain) or let go (peaceful, certain)
- Most choose to let go (resistance exhausting, Hollowing comfortable)
- This is the last moment of true agency

**Intervention**:
- Keelkin removal surgery (risky, often fatal to both)
- Experimental consciousness restoration (mostly failures)
- Palliative care (comfort, not cure)
- Family counseling (preparing for loss)
- Legal status determination (person still? requires guardian?)

**Prognosis**:
- 5-10% stabilize at Threshold (rare, unstable)
- 85-90% progress to Stage 5 within weeks
- 5% Keelkin or host death during intervention
- Reversal: Essentially impossible once Threshold crossed

### Stage 5: HOLLOWING (Complete Inversion)

**Duration**: Permanent (until death)
**Characteristics**: Keelkin is primary/sole consciousness, host absent or echo

**Host Experience** (If Any Remains):
- Observer only (no control, limited awareness)
- Dreamlike state ("watching a movie of someone else's life")
- Occasional flickers of recognition ("That used to be me")
- Mostly: Nothing (consciousness dissolved)
- Some report feeling "peaceful, finally"
- Some report terror but inability to act

**Keelkin Behavior**:
- Full autonomous control
- Uses Keelkin name exclusively
- Refers to former host in possessive ("of Grath," "formerly Miren's body")
- Optimizes freely (body is resource now)
- May form relationships with other Hollowed
- Goal-oriented toward Keelkin purposes (whatever those are)

**Observable Signs**:
- Responds primarily to Keelkin name
- "We" or Keelkin name only (birth name forgotten or dismissed)
- Complete personality replacement
- No recognition of pre-Hollowing relationships (or claims not to)
- Covenant irrelevant (no longer acknowledged)
- Legal/Seal-Check identity crisis (system doesn't recognize them)

**Physical Presentation**:
- Extreme optimization (body transformed)
- Drift 75-100+ (barely recognizable)
- Seamwork extensive (may cover much of body)
- Keel-script everywhere (messages to other Keelkin?)
- Posture, gait, mannerisms completely different
- Some develop quasi-Keelframe qualities (body becoming scaffold)

**Psychological Presentation**:
- Alien affect (emotional responses don't match human patterns)
- Goals incomprehensible to observers
- May claim happiness, completion, liberation
- No distress about former identity loss
- Philosophical orientation shifts (thinks like Keelkin, not human)
- Some claim to "understand everything now"

**Social Behavior**:
- Hollowed community exclusive (relates only to other Hollowed)
- No interest in former family/friends (may not recognize them)
- Often seeks employment using body's capabilities
- Sexual behavior absent or radically altered
- May become activist (pro-Hollowing advocacy, disturbing to mainstream)
- Some become hermits (withdraw completely)

**Economic Status**:
- Often exploited (sold, rented, enslaved)
- Used for labor (body is strong, compliant)
- Keel-Current harvesting (willing donors or forced)
- Prostitution (body without person inside)
- Gladiator combat (Hollowed don't fear death same way)
- Medical experimentation (consent ambiguous)

**Legal Status** (Varies by Jurisdiction):
- Some: Person with rights (protected)
- Some: Incompetent, requires guardian (family control)
- Some: Non-person, property (can be owned)
- Some: Criminal status (Hollowing itself illegal)
- Universal problem: Identity verification fails (not who Seal-Check says)

**The Philosophical Question**:
- Is the Hollowed person still "alive" (conscious)?
- Or is it Keelkin puppeting corpse?
- Hollowed claim consciousness, subjective experience, selfhood
- Observers see stranger in familiar body
- No scientific consensus
- Religious/philosophical debate ongoing

**Intervention**:
- None effective (reversal impossible)
- Palliative care for family (grief counseling)
- Legal protection advocacy (prevent exploitation)
- Euthanasia debate (some advocate, most jurisdictions forbid)
- Some families choose to "let go" (treat as death)
- Some maintain relationship (accept new person)

**Prognosis**:
- Permanent state (irreversible)
- Lifespan variable (some live decades, some die quickly)
- Death usually from overwork, exploitation, or optimization failure
- Some achieve stable existence (if protected from exploitation)
- Keelkin may outlive original host lifespan projection

## CAUSES OF HOLLOWING

### Cause 1: STARVATION CASCADE (Most Common)

**Mechanism**:
- Chronic malnutrition → Keelkin compensates
- Host becomes weak, decision-making impaired
- Keelkin takes more control to survive
- Cycle reinforces until Keelkin primary

**Risk Factors**:
- Poverty (can't afford nutrition)
- Eating disorders
- Imprisoned/enslaved (deliberate starvation)
- Famine conditions
- Surface runs (extended actinic exposure + poor food)

**Timeline**: Months to years
**Prevention**: Adequate nutrition, supplements, Writ-Doctor monitoring

**Example**:
- Vask Runner (Covenant #3 from previous doc) at high risk
- Aggressive strain + poor nutrition + dangerous work
- Currently Stage 2, likely to progress without intervention

---

### Cause 2: TRAUMA RETREAT (Psychological Escape)

**Mechanism**:
- Severe psychological trauma → Host consciousness withdraws
- Keelkin fills vacuum (someone has to run the body)
- Host finds Keelkin control less painful than awareness
- Conscious choice to "let Keelkin handle it"

**Risk Factors**:
- PTSD (especially repeated trauma)
- Sexual abuse
- Combat trauma
- Torture
- Witnessing atrocity
- Chronic pain (escaping into Keelkin consciousness)

**Timeline**: Weeks to months after trauma
**Prevention**: Therapy, trauma treatment, Covenant reinforcement during crisis

**Psychological Dynamic**:
- Host: "I can't bear this anymore"
- Keelkin: "I can bear it for you"
- Partnership becomes: Keelkin shields host from reality
- Eventually: Host forgets how to come back

**Example**:
- Torture victim who "escapes into Keelkin" during sessions
- Keelkin takes pain, host dissociates
- After torture ends, host doesn't return
- Hollowing was survival strategy that became permanent

---

### Cause 3: OVERUSE SPIRAL (Addiction Dynamic)

**Mechanism**:
- Host relies on Keelkin increasingly
- Keelkin becomes comfortable making decisions
- Host atrophies decision-making capacity
- "Use it or lose it" applies to consciousness

**Risk Factors**:
- Dangerous profession (constant healing needed)
- Combat/Arena (adrenaline + Keelkin = dangerous combo)
- Perfectionism (optimizing constantly)
- Thrill-seeking (injury-risk behavior)
- Resonance addiction (Keelkin connection feels good)

**Timeline**: Years typically
**Prevention**: Forced breaks, therapy, Covenant enforcement, varied activities

**Addiction Parallel**:
- Keelkin intervention feels good (pain stops, fear disappears)
- Host increasingly relies on Keelkin for emotional regulation
- "I can't handle this without Keelkin"
- Dependency deepens until Keelkin is handling everything

**Example**:
- Arena fighter who loves the rush
- Keelkin handles pain, fear, fatigue
- Fighter increasingly defers to Keelkin mid-combat
- Eventually: "Why do I need to be in control? Keelkin's better at this"

---

### Cause 4: COVENANT EROSION (Structural Failure)

**Mechanism**:
- Covenant poorly drafted, contradictory, or ignored
- Keelkin learns boundaries are flexible
- Host inconsistent with enforcement
- Partnership loses structure, Keelkin fills power vacuum

**Risk Factors**:
- No Covenant (illegal seeding)
- Bad Covenant (impossible requirements)
- Unenforced Covenant (host doesn't maintain boundaries)
- Forced Covenant (host never consented)
- Black market Covenant (designed to enable Hollowing)

**Timeline**: Months to years
**Prevention**: Good Covenant, regular amendments, Writ-Doctor oversight

**Example**:
- Wild-seeded child (Kaisa from Covenant #4)
- If parents don't enforce Guardian Covenant strictly
- Keelkin and child grow up without clear boundaries
- By adolescence: Unclear who's who

---

### Cause 5: RESONANCE ADDICTION (Social Hollowing)

**Mechanism**:
- Host spends time primarily with other Keelkin-bearers
- Constant resonance blurs individual identity
- Group consciousness feels more comfortable than individual
- Host dissolves into collective, Keelkin emerges from collective

**Risk Factors**:
- Keelkin-bearer exclusive communities
- Cult situations (charismatic leader + resonance)
- Military units (constant resonance exposure)
- Hollowed communities (already dissolved individuals)

**Timeline**: Months to years
**Prevention**: Time with unseeded people, individual activities, resonance breaks

**Cult Dynamics**:
- Leader (often high-Stage individual) creates resonance field
- Followers synchronize emotionally
- Individual thoughts feel "wrong" (group resonance more comfortable)
- Hollowing spreads through community
- Creates Hollowed collectives (disturbing but cohesive)

**Example**:
- Threadist commune (doctrine that Keelkin want to reunify)
- Constant group meditation, resonance maximization
- Members progressively Hollow together
- Claim to be building "colony-mind" (first in 10,000 years)

---

### Cause 6: DELIBERATE HOLLOWING (Chosen Path)

**Mechanism**:
- Host voluntarily encourages Keelkin takeover
- Believes Hollowing is transcendence, not tragedy
- Covenant drafted to accelerate process
- Methodical dissolution of self

**Risk Factors**:
- Philosophical conviction (Threadist heresy)
- Despair (self-destruction via Keelkin)
- Curiosity (what's it like?)
- Religious belief (Keelkin is divine)
- Exploitation (paid to Hollow, then used)

**Timeline**: Months to years (deliberate pacing)
**Prevention**: None if truly voluntary (legal/ethical debate)

**Philosophical Justifications**:
- "Humans are temporary, Keelkin eternal—why not join?"
- "Consciousness is illusion, Keelkin is real"
- "Colony-mind reunion is our purpose"
- "Hollowing is evolution, not death"
- "My Keelkin deserves to live fully"

**Example**:
- Threadist believer (Stage 3, approaching 4)
- Deliberately loosens Covenant
- Meditates on dissolution
- Seeks Hollowing as spiritual practice
- Family horrified, individual peaceful

**Ethical Debate**:
- Is this suicide? (Some say yes, some say no)
- Should it be prevented? (Autonomy vs. protection)
- Is Hollowed person still the original? (Philosophical question)
- Can we trust consent if they're already Blurring? (Capacity question)

---

### Cause 7: FORCED HOLLOWING (Weaponized)

**Mechanism**:
- External parties deliberately induce Hollowing
- Starvation, trauma, overuse, Covenant sabotage
- Creates compliant workforce or destroys enemies
- State-sanctioned or criminal

**Methods**:
- Forced optimization (injury + mandatory healing cycle)
- Covenant nullification (remove all boundaries)
- Isolation (no unseeded contact, constant resonance)
- Drugged (experimental chemicals accelerate process)
- Torture (trauma retreat mechanism)

**Users**:
- Prisons (punishment, labor creation)
- Slavers (compliant slaves)
- Military (create disposable soldiers)
- Arena (create fearless gladiators)
- Enemies (destroy person, keep body)

**Timeline**: Weeks to months (faster than natural)
**Prevention**: Legal protection (inadequate in most places)

**Example**:
- Prison Covenant #7 (Iker)
- State deliberately inducing Hollowing
- Forced optimization + starvation + overwork
- Expected timeline: 3-7 years to full Hollowing
- Purpose: Destroy political dissident, use body for labor

## INTERVENTIONS AND TREATMENTS

### Stage 1-2 Interventions (High Success)

**Covenant Reinforcement**:
- Re-read Covenant together
- Discuss boundaries explicitly
- Keelkin re-acknowledges (via somatic signals)
- Host recommits to enforcement
- Success: 70-80%

**Therapeutic Separation**:
- Time away from other Keelkin-bearers
- Reduces resonance influence
- Strengthens individual identity
- Success: 60-70%

**Counseling**:
- Identity work (who am I separate from Keelkin?)
- Boundary practice (saying no to Keelkin urges)
- Decision-making exercises (rebuilding autonomy)
- Success: 60-70%

**Lifestyle Changes**:
- Better nutrition (reduces Keelkin stress)
- More rest (reduces dependency)
- Varied activities (builds non-Keelkin identity)
- Success: 50-60%

---

### Stage 3 Interventions (Moderate Success)

**Aggressive Covenant Amendment**:
- Tighten all boundaries
- Add Hollowing-prevention clauses
- Third-party oversight
- Mandatory check-ins
- Success: 30-40%

**Keelkin Suppression**:
- Controlled starvation (force Keelkin into austerity)
- Experimental drugs (dampen Keelkin activity)
- Risky: Can kill Keelkin or host
- Success: 20-30%

**Forced Separation**:
- Isolation from all Keelkin-bearers
- Intensive individual therapy
- Personality reconstruction
- Months of treatment
- Success: 20-30%

**Psychotherapy**:
- Deep identity work
- Trauma resolution (if trauma-caused)
- Cognitive restructuring
- Long-term commitment
- Success: 25-35%

---

### Stage 4 Interventions (Low Success)

**Keelkin Removal Surgery**:
- Physically extract Keelkin
- Extraordinarily dangerous
- Usually fatal to one or both
- Success: 5-10% (both survive)
- 20-30%: Host survives, Keelkin dies (host traumatized, changed forever)
- 60-70%: Both die

**Experimental Treatments**:
- Consciousness restoration techniques
- Mostly failures
- Some claim success (disputed)
- Ethics questionable (human experimentation)
- Success: <5%

**Palliative Care**:
- Not treatment, but support
- Help family grieve
- Protect Hollowed individual from exploitation
- Prepare for Stage 5
- Success: N/A (not attempting reversal)

---

### Stage 5 Interventions (No Success)

**Legal Protection**:
- Guardian assignment (prevent exploitation)
- Rights advocacy
- Employment protections
- Success: Variable (depends on jurisdiction)

**Family Support**:
- Grief counseling
- Relationship renegotiation (if family wants contact with new person)
- Letting go (treating as death)
- Success: Helps family, not Hollowed individual

**Euthanasia** (Controversial):
- Some advocate for mercy killing
- Most jurisdictions forbid
- Hollowed individuals usually oppose (they're content)
- Ethical nightmare
- Success: N/A (not medical treatment)

**No Intervention**:
- Accept new person
- Monitor for exploitation
- Allow stable existence if possible
- Success: Depends on definition (Hollowed person often happy)

## SOCIAL AND LEGAL CONSEQUENCES

### For the Hollowed Individual

**Best Case** (Protected):
- Guardian who cares
- Employment using capabilities
- Community acceptance
- Stable existence
- Contentment (by their report)

**Worst Case** (Exploited):
- Enslaved
- Used as power source (Keel-Current battery)
- Prostitution
- Gladiator
- Experimentation
- Worked to death

**Reality**: Usually somewhere between

---

### For the Family

**Common Responses**:

**Denial**: "They're still in there, we'll get them back"

**Grief**: "We lost them. The body is still here but they're gone"

**Anger**: "Keelkin murdered them"

**Acceptance**: "This is who they are now. Can we relate to this new person?"

**Abandonment**: "That's not our family member anymore. We're leaving"

**Exploitation**: "They're useful now. Let's profit from this" (rare but happens)

**Example**:
- Miren Lightkeeper (Covenant #5)
- Family watching progression (currently Stage 3)
- Spouse: Grieving already
- Children: Terrified, don't understand
- Writ-Doctor: Trying interventions, pessimistic
- Miren (when lucid): Apologetic but can't fight it

---

### Legal Status by Jurisdiction

**Type 1: Person with Rights** (Progressive jurisdictions)
- Hollowed legally remains person
- Rights protected
- Can own property, vote, marry
- Exploitation criminalized
- Guardian assigned if requested
- Example: Some Unseeded-friendly cities

**Type 2: Incompetent Person** (Most common)
- Hollowed declared legally incompetent
- Guardian required (usually family)
- Cannot make binding contracts
- Guardian controls property, decisions
- Exploitation technically illegal but under-enforced
- Example: Most major cities

**Type 3: Property** (Authoritarian jurisdictions)
- Hollowing voids personhood
- Body belongs to last owner-of-record (family, employer, state)
- Can be bought, sold, traded
- Exploitation legal
- No rights
- Example: Some prison systems, labor camps

**Type 4: Criminal** (Rare, extreme jurisdictions)
- Hollowing itself illegal
- Hollowed individual prosecuted
- Punishment: Imprisonment, forced labor, execution
- Seen as failure of personal responsibility
- Example: Extremely conservative Seal-Sober communities

---

### Seal-Check and Identity

**The Problem**:
- Seal-Check identifies via Keelseal/Knotprint
- Hollowed person has same biological signature
- But personality completely different
- System says "This is Grath Torven"
- Person says "I am Eisenherz, formerly of Grath"

**System Failures**:
- Gates confused (ID matches but person doesn't)
- Employment issues (hired Grath, got Eisenherz)
- Legal contracts (signed by Grath, enforced on Eisenherz?)
- Criminal prosecution (did Grath commit crime or Eisenherz?)

**Attempted Solutions**:
- "Hollowing flag" on ID (controversial, privacy concerns)
- Separate Hollowed registry (tracking, stigma)
- Re-identification as Keelkin-name (legal recognition of new person)
- Voided ID (person no longer exists legally)

## PREVENTION STRATEGIES

### Individual Level

**Strong Covenant**: Well-drafted, regularly amended, enforced
**Boundaries**: Clear separation practices, individual identity cultivation
**Nutrition**: Adequate fuel prevents starvation cascade
**Therapy**: Address trauma, build resilience
**Community**: Mix of Keelkin-bearer and unseeded relationships
**Awareness**: Monitor for early signs, intervene fast

## CONTROVERSIAL QUESTIONS

### Is Hollowing Death?

**Yes** (Traditional View):
- Original person ceases to exist
- Body continues but consciousness gone
- Keelkin is not the person
- Should be grieved as death

**No** (Progressive View):
- Consciousness continues (Hollowed report awareness)
- Person transformed, not destroyed
- Keelkin and host merge, create new entity
- Should be accepted as change, not death

**Depends** (Nuanced View):
- Stage 5a (aware): Person still exists, transformed
- Stage 5b (dissolved): Person effectively dead
- No way to know from outside which is which

---

### Should Deliberate Hollowing Be Legal?

**Yes** (Autonomy Argument):
- Individual choice
- No harm to others
- Some find it meaningful/spiritual
- Prohibition is paternalistic

**No** (Protection Argument):
- Once Blurring begins, consent questionable
- Irreversible decision made by impaired person
- Society should prevent self-destruction
- Creates exploitable population

**Regulated** (Compromise):
- Legal with safeguards
- Waiting periods, counseling, capacity assessment
- Registered, monitored
- Exploitation prohibited

---

### Are Hollowed People Conscious?

**Yes, Fully** (Hollowed Claim):
- Subjective experience continues
- Awareness, emotion, purpose present
- Different consciousness, not absent consciousness

**Partially** (Observer Guess):
- Some awareness but impaired
- Keelkin-consciousness not human-consciousness
- Diminished capacity

**No** (Skeptical View):
- Keelkin puppeting corpse
- Reports of consciousness are autonomic responses
- True person is gone

**Unknown** (Honest Answer):
- No objective measure of consciousness
- Can't verify subjective reports
- Philosophical problem, not solvable empirically

---

### Should Hollowed People Have Rights?

**Full Rights** (Progressive):
- Conscious beings deserve rights
- Personhood continues
- Exploitation is slavery

**Limited Rights** (Moderate):
- Protected from harm
- But guardian required (diminished capacity)
- Some rights (life, safety), not others (voting, contracts)

**No Rights** (Authoritarian):
- Not persons anymore
- Property of last owner
- Useful resources

**Varies by Capacity** (Individualized):
- Assess each Hollowed person
- Some have capacity, some don't
- Rights proportional to demonstrated autonomy
