## WRITING WITH THEMATIC AWARENESS

### Power/Cost Scene Checklist
- [ ] What capability is being used in this scene?
- [ ] What is the immediate cost? (resources, time, exposure)
- [ ] What is the accumulated cost? (drift, seamwork, debt)
- [ ] Who pays? Who profits?
- [ ] Is the ledger visible on a body?

### Rebellion/Empire Scene Checklist
- [ ] What institution controls access in this scene?
- [ ] Who benefits from that control?
- [ ] What would bypass/subvert/destroy that control?
- [ ] Is someone's autonomy bounded? By whom?
- [ ] Who decides what this body is for?

### Combined Thematic Pressure
The most powerful Orrovae scenes occur when both axes converge:
- A character gains power at visible cost AND
- That power either serves or undermines empire AND
- The cost reveals what the empire extracts AND
- Rebellion becomes the only path to stop paying
