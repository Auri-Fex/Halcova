# Spore Crystals

## Description
Spore Crystals are rare, naturally occurring mineralized clusters of mycelium found deep underground or in ancient ruins. They store immense bioelectric and magical energy, making them highly sought after in the world of mycothurgy.

## Uses
- Power source for magitech devices, advanced spellcasting, and fungal constructs
- Can be consumed to instantly replenish Spore Points or fuel large-scale magic
- Essential for the operation of spore-powered vehicles, living buildings, and bioengineered tools

## Scarcity & Value
- Only found in specific biomes or after rare fungal events
- Overharvesting can destabilize local ecosystems or attract dangerous entities
- Controlling Spore Crystal mines is a source of political and economic power

## Mechanics
- Limited supply; tracked as a resource in campaigns
- Can be refined or split for different uses
- May have unique properties based on origin (e.g., color, energy output, side effects)

## World Impact
- Factions and individuals compete for access and control
- Spore Crystals are central to technological and magical advancement
- Their scarcity drives exploration, conflict, and innovation
