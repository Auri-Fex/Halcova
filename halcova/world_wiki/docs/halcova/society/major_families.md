# Major Families

---
**Story Reference:**
This section is based on "The Reading" (Còcalnòc family’s fraud), "The Constrained" (Vonalnòc family’s struggle), and "The Warden" (exposure of family secrets). Vas-Eròcal and Szòvòtar Hàlmahtfhì’s line appear in supporting roles across the stories.

**Implications in the Stories:**
- The stories show how family legacy is both a source of power and a site of vulnerability, with scandals and secrets shaping the fate of entire lineages.
---

## The Còcalnòc Family

A merchant dynasty whose fortunes have been built on generations of purchased patterns. Central to recent scandals involving pattern fraud and the shadow ledger.

## The Vonalnòc Family

A Hand Quarter family, marked by constrained patterns and a history of Pattern House service. Their story is entwined with the exposure of silencing orders and the cost of truth.

## Other Notable Families

- Vas-Eròcal: Guild leaders, implicated in pattern adjustments.
- Szòvòtar Hàlmahtfhì’s line: Pattern readers with a legacy of both service and corruption.
