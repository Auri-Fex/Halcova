# Minor Settlements and Frontier

> **Quick Summary**: Hundreds of small settlements (100-5,000 population) dot the habitable zones, serving as mines, farms, waypoints, and refuges, with high mortality and constant threat.

---

## Settlement Types

**Mining Outposts** (Sulenor, Tavanor, Vashor):
- Extract resources
- High fatality (2-5 year lifespan for workers)
- Company-owned
- Minimal infrastructure

**Farming Communities** (Tavanor, Eshtor):
- Grow food (fungal crops, limited agriculture)
- Family-based
- Vulnerable to raids
- Sustenance level

**Border Fortresses** (All regions):
- Military outposts
- Defend territory
- Small garrisons (50-500 soldiers)
- Strategic locations

**Nomadic Camps** (Vashor, Konor):
- Temporary settlements
- Follow herds or resources
- Clan-based
- High mobility

**Forest Villages** (Eshtor):
- Hidden from extractionists
- Sustainable practices
- Guerrilla bases
- Allied with Keepers (sometimes)

**Refugee Camps** (Borders, especially near Konor):
- Overcrowded
- Disease-ridden
- Desperate violence
- Humanitarian crisis

---

## Survival Challenges

**Environmental**:
- Exposure to cold
- Distance from aid
- Limited resources
- Creature threats

**Human**:
- Bandit raids
- Military conflicts
- Isolation
- No law enforcement

**Economic**:
- Poverty universal
- No services
- Self-sufficient or die
- Exploitation by barons
