# Kalen-Sheloth ("Bright Hold")

> **Quick Summary**: The continent's largest city (900,000) and political capital, home to Shalen-Mor's palace, the Arcane Academy, and brutal class inequality.

**Population**: 900,000
**Ruler**: Shalen-Mor (High Archon)
**Region**: Central Tavanor
**Founded**: Ancient (1,000+ years)

---

## Districts

**Palace Quarter** (50,000): Elite, nobles, high mages
**Academic District** (100,000): University, libraries, scholars
**Commercial Center** (150,000): Markets, banking, trade
**Industrial Zone** (200,000): Factories, refineries, workers
**Slums** (400,000): Tenements, disease, desperation

---

## Landmarks

- **Sky Beacon**: Navigation landmark, visible 100km
- **Council Hall**: Nine barons meet here
- **Arcane Academy**: 1,000 elite students
- **Palace**: Shalen-Mor's fortress, 200 rooms
- **Undercity**: Ancient ruins beneath

---

## Economy

Government, academia, trade, banking, rail hub

---

## Dangers

Political assassinations, Breakers bombings, class riots, disease, Inquisition
