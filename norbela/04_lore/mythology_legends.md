# Mythology and Legends

> **Quick Summary**: Cultural stories, creation myths, and legends that shape how different peoples understand their world and its magical nature.

---

## Creation Myths

### The Bleeding Heart

**Origin**: Widespread folk belief
**Story**: 
- Meshvalt moon is heart torn from ancient god
- Eruptions = divine blood
- Rings = god's tears
- Magic = both gift and curse

**Implication**: Over-extraction = bleeding god dry

### The Frozen Prison

**Origin**: Eshtor shamanic tradition
**Story**:
- World is prison for ancient demons
- Magic = demon essence leaking
- Extraction = freeing demons gradually
- Dead zones = where demons escaped

**Implication**: Using magic is dangerous hubris

### The Cycle

**Origin**: Konor mystics
**Story**:
- World dies and rebirths
- Current age just one of many
- Entropy = natural death phase
- New world will come after

**Implication**: Accept doom; prepare for rebirth

### The Sacrifice

**Origin**: Faith of Fire
**Story**:
- Gods gave magic at great cost
- Gift comes with responsibility
- Overuse = betraying gift
- Dead zones = divine punishment

**Implication**: Extraction is sin; we deserve doom

## Cultural Legends

### The Seventh Continent

**Status**: Possibly true
**Story**:
- Ancient texts mention "seventh and eighth" continents
- Consumed by dead zones long ago
- Complete extinction
- We're next

### The Yellow Sun

**Status**: Unknown
**Story**:
- Oral traditions speak of "yellow sun" (not blue Blaysol)
- "Warmer times"
- "Green world"
- Dismissed as fantasy

**Question**: Ancestral memory of different world? Or mythology?

### The Lost Archive

**Status**: Possibly real
**Story**:
- Pre-collapse library in Vashor glacier
- Contains all knowledge
- Location lost
- Many seek; none find

### The Perfect Device

**Status**: Myth? Warning?
**Story**:
- Ancients built device that worked perfectly
- Reversed entropy successfully
- Then destroyed it
- Warning: "Not all problems have solutions"

## Regional Folklore

### Tavanor

**Ghost Cities**:
- Cities in dead zones still "alive"
- Phantom lights and sounds
- Ghosts of those who died in Cascade
- Stay away

### Sulenor

**The First Wound**:
- Volcano crater = where world first bled magic
- Actually ancient impact site
- Pilgrimage destination
- Mutations attributed to divine test

### Eshtor

**The Keepers' Oath**:
- Vornalen-Keepers made pact with forest spirits
- Kill outsiders to protect sacred groves
- Death = joining the forest forever
- Some truth (religious devotion drives behavior)

### Vashor

**Ice Demons**:
- Massive ice creatures are demons
- Sent to test humanity
- Killing them honorable
- Actually just animals

### Konor

**The Weapon**:
- Ancient superweapon buried in ruins
- Will save Konor
- Or destroy everyone
- Meshira-Tal searching (may be true)

## Prophecies and Omens

### The End Times

**Various Versions**:
- Dead zones consume all (happening)
- Blue sun goes dark (not happening... yet)
- Moon stops erupting (not happening)
- New age begins after death (hope)

### Signs of Doom

**People Believe These Mean End**:
- Aurora colors wrong
- Creatures acting strange
- Unnatural weather
- Mass deaths
- All happening now

### The Savior

**Various Traditions Expect**:
- Hero who will save world
- Device that reverses entropy
- Divine intervention
- Return of ancient power

**Reality**: No savior coming

## In the World

**How Mythology Functions**:
- Explains the inexplicable
- Provides meaning
- Offers hope (false)
- Controls behavior (sometimes)

**Class Differences**:
- Elite: Know myths are stories
- Middle: Uncertain what to believe
- Underclass: Cling to myths for comfort
