# Major Cities (Non-Capital)

> **Quick Summary**: Secondary cities serve as regional hubs, ports, academic centers, industrial zones, or frontier outposts, each with 50,000-200,000 population.

---

## Telian-Kresh (Tavanor)

**Population**: 150,000
**Type**: Coastal port city

**Features**:
- Arcane shipyards
- Fishing industry
- Lighthouse
- Nethvor-controlled smuggling hub

**Economy**: Trade, fishing, shipbuilding
**Dangers**: Pirates, smugglers, storms

---

## Gen-Sorath (Tavanor)

**Population**: 80,000 (under siege, starving)
**Type**: Academic mountain city

**Features**:
- Telumesh Archive (1 million+ texts)
- Crystal mines
- Arcane defenses (holding)

**Current Crisis**: Inquisition siege (1 month), food running out, assault imminent
**Economy**: Scholarship, crystal mining

---

## Shira-Nesh (Sulenor/Konor border)

**Population**: 50,000 (declining)
**Type**: Frontier salvage town

**Features**:
- Black market
- Refugee influx
- Warlord territories
- Desperate violence

**Economy**: Scavenging, smuggling, human trafficking
**Dangers**: Entropy storms, bandits, lawlessness

---

## The First Grove (Eshtor - Hidden)

**Population**: ~5,000-10,000 (estimated)
**Type**: Vornalen-Keeper sanctuary

**Features**:
- Built into mega-trees
- Creature sanctuaries
- Shamanic training grounds
- Location unknown to outsiders

**Access**: Kill outsiders on sight
**Culture**: Indigenous warriors, druids
