# Food and Dining Culture

> **Quick Summary**: What you eat reveals your casteâ€”from elaborate multi-course banquets for elite to garbage scavenging for outcasts.

---

## By Caste

### Upper Castes (Shyakor)

**Meals**: 3+ elaborate daily  
**Foods**: Imported spices, tropical fruits, creature meats, aged wines  
**Dining**: Multi-course, servants, entertainment  
**Cost**: Single meal = Korbal's yearly wages

### Middle Castes (Meshkorin)

**Meals**: 2 daily (breakfast, dinner)  
**Foods**: Bread, cheese, preserved meats, vegetables, beer, tea  
**Dining**: Family tables, modest but sufficient  
**Cost**: 10-15% of income

### Lower Castes (Telkorin/Korbal)

**Meals**: 1 meal + scraps if lucky  
**Foods**: Gruel, bread, spoiled meat, moldy vegetables, rats  
**Dining**: Eat standing/sitting on floors  
**Cost**: 50-60% of income (still not enough)

### Indentured/Slaves/Outcasts

**Meals**: Whatever available  
**Foods**: Garbage, animal feed, bones, insects, foraged plants  
**Cost**: N/A (begging, stealing, scavenging)

---

## Common Foods

**Shibak Stew**: Worker food; fungal roots + preserved fish + grains; filling, bland

**Vorn Bread**: Dark bread from forest lichens; cheap, nutritious, bitter

**Shyak-Infused Spirits**: Magically-charged alcohol; expensive, addictive, hallucinogenic
