# Crime and Punishment

---

## By Caste

### Lower Castes Commit

**Crimes**: Theft, fraud, assault, illegal gathering, trespassing

**Punishment**:
- Beating
- Mutilation (hand cut off for theft)
- Indenture
- Execution (hanging, burning)

### Middle Castes Commit

**Crimes**: Fraud, bribery, assault, smuggling

**Punishment**:
- Heavy fines
- Guild expulsion
- Indenture (rare)
- Execution (only treason/murder)

### Upper Castes Commit

**Crimes**: Everything (murder, torture, rape, slavery, treason)

**Punishment**:
- Rarely prosecuted
- Money buys immunity
- House arrest (if caught by other barons)
- Execution (only if baron demands, political)

---

## Informal Justice

**Vigilante**: Common in lower castes  
**Gang Justice**: Criminal organizations enforce own rules  
**Mob Violence**: Riots and lynchings  
**Rebel Actions**: Morshan-Breakers execute "oppressors"
