# Finyak Substrate

> **Quick Summary**: Finyak is the primordial arcane mineral or energy field deep beneath Norbela's surface—the true wellspring of all magic, ancient and mysterious, that only magical creatures can metabolize into usable power.

**In this article:**
- What is Finyak?
- Distribution and Density
- Atmospheric Presence
- Depletion and Regeneration
- Relationship to Magic
- Scientific vs Mystical Views

---

## What is Finyak?

**Definition**: Rare mineral or energy field suffusing planet's crust, mantle, and atmosphere with arcane potential
**Age**: Ancient, possibly predating all life
**State**: Solid crystalline form underground; energy/particle form in atmosphere
**Accessibility**: Cannot be directly mined or extracted by humans or technology

### Physical Properties

- Crystalline structure when concentrated
- Energy field when diffuse
- Responds to life (especially magical creatures)
- Self-organizing into veins and concentrations
- Affected by geological activity

## Distribution and Density

### Geographic Variation

**Highest Concentrations**:
- Crystal Mantle Ranges (Stanyak Ral)
- Volcanic zones (Sulenor)
- Ancient forests (Eshtor)
- Subglacial river junctions

**Moderate Levels**:
- Most temperate regions
- Underground cave systems
- Coastal areas
- Active geological zones

**Depleted Zones**:
- Dead zones (shisolak)
- Over-extracted areas
- Ancient collapsed regions
- Entropy wastelands

### Depth Profile

- **Surface**: Trace amounts from atmospheric fallout
- **Shallow (0-1km)**: Moderate; accessible to plant roots and burrowing creatures
- **Mid-depth (1-10km)**: Highest concentrations; primary veins
- **Deep (10km+)**: Dense deposits but inaccessible

---

## Atmospheric Presence

### Ring Particle Infall

Primary source of airborne finyak is magical rain from rings—finyak-rich particles from moon eruptions entering atmosphere.

**Process**:
1. Moon erupts finyak-crystal material
2. Forms rings around planet
3. Particles spiral inward
4. Enter atmosphere as magical dust
5. Creates baseline magical field

**Distribution**:
- Global coverage but not uniform
- Equatorial regions receive more
- Weather patterns redistribute
- Settles into geological features

### Seasonal Variation

- **High Magic Season**: Maximum infall from frequent eruptions
- **Low Magic Season**: Reduced supply as eruptions decrease
- **Transitions**: Unstable atmospheric finyak levels

---

## Depletion and Regeneration

### Natural Cycles

**Regeneration**:
- Volcanic activity brings deep finyak to surface
- Ring infall replenishes atmospheric supply
- Geological processes concentrate deposits
- Time scale: Thousands to millions of years

**Depletion**:
- Magical creature extraction (natural, sustainable rate)
- Over-harvesting by civilization (accelerated)
- Entropy spread (conversion to dead zones)
- Magical disasters (cascades)

### Current Crisis

**Status**: Net depletion globally
**Rate**: 40% of land now dead zones; expanding 10% per year
**Cause**: Industrial-scale extraction exceeds regeneration
**Prognosis**: Terminal decline without intervention

## Relationship to Magic

### The Energy Chain

1. **Finyak** exists in substrate and atmosphere
2. **Magical creatures** metabolize it via symbiotic microfauna
3. **Shyak** (arcane oil) produced by microbes
4. **Devices** burn shyak for magical effects
5. **Byproducts** pollute or contribute to entropy

### Why Only Creatures?

**Key Principle**: Humans and technology cannot directly access finyak

**Biological Requirement**:
- Specialized symbiotic microbes needed
- Metabolic conversion process
- Organic template for magic
- No artificial substitute found

**This Means**:
- All usable magic comes from creatures
- Technology entirely dependent on biological harvest
- Extinction of magical creatures = end of magic
- Conservation critical for civilization survival
