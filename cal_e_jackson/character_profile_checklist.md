# Character Profile Checklist

For each character (POV and NPC), include:

- Name
- Role in story (POV/NPC)
- Background & history
- Personality traits
- Motivations & goals
- Dialogue style (speech patterns, vocabulary, tone)
- Humor & Irony Style (default is dark/sarcastic, but specify if different)
- Manipulation/persuasion style
- Relationships (key connections, alliances, betrayals, mentorships, rivalries, romances)
- Emotional triggers
- Tragic flaws & internal conflicts (pride, fear, obsession, guilt, naivety, etc.)
- Character arc summary (setup, inciting incident, rising tension, climax, resolution)
- Unique quirks or habits
- Mood/Atmosphere Sensitivity (how POV affects sensory cues, mood, and atmosphere)
- Symbolism & Foreshadowing Awareness (is the character attuned to symbolic/metaphorical details or foreshadowing?)
- Layered Details (POV-driven personal observations about environment)
- Worldbuilding Perception (which cultural practices, architecture, rituals, technologies, etc., does the character notice/value?)
- Emotional Filters (how mood, trauma, or personality shape perception)
- Costs Worn (physical, emotional, psychological, mental)
- Scene card references

Add more elements as needed for your story or world.
