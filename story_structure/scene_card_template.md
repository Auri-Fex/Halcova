## Scene Structure Checklist (for each scene)

- Foreshadowing: Are hints/setups present and clear?
- Subplots: Which subplots are advanced or referenced?
- Character Arcs: How does the protagonist/supporting cast change?
- Theme Integration: What themes are present or developed?
- Emotional Arc: What are the emotional highs/lows?
- Conflicts & Stakes: What’s at risk and who is involved?
- Subtext & Layers: What’s unsaid or implied?
- Payoff Notes: What setups are paid off or planted?
- Connections to econometrics, behavioral economics, game theory, decision theory, philosophy, psychology: Are these concepts present or influencing the scene?
- Pacing & Rhythm: Is the intended pacing clear and effective?
- Transition & Flow: Are scene transitions smooth and engaging?
- Worldbuilding Consistency: Are new details introduced and continuity maintained?
- POV Reliability: Is the POV reliable, unreliable, or intentionally misleading?
- Audience Engagement: Does the scene raise questions or curiosity?
- Revision History: Are major changes and feedback tracked?
